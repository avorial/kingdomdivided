# **Major Guilds of the Leandan League**

- **Armorersmiths: **Armrors, Automations, Machinists
- **Brewers: **Brewers, Distillers, Vitners
- **Culinarian: **Bakers, Butchers, Cooking
- **Farmers: **Farming, Husbandary
- **Glasssmith: **Glass Blowers**, **Glassware, Lensers
- **Harmony: **Undertakers, Guides (therapists), Arbiters (lawyers), Assassins (secret!)
- **Jewelers:  **Gemist, Gem Cutter, Jewelers
- **Leatherworkers: **Leatherers, Tanners
- **Parnassians: **Actors, Painters, Musicians, Scuplters, Singers, Storytellers
- **Physicians:  **Apothecaries (pharma), Boffins (chemists), Doctors, Midwives, Veterinarians
- **Scribes: **Bookbinders, Cartographers, Illuminators, Public Servants, Teachers
- **Miners: **Masons, Miners, Stonecutters
- **Shipwrights: **Airships, Fishing, Navigators, Netmakers, Sailmakers, Sailors (air  and sea), Whalers
- **Smiths: **Finesmiths, Locksmiths (anything *not* armor or weapon making)
- **Tailors: **Cobblers, Pattern Drafters, Seamstress, Tailors

- **Weaponsmiths: **Bladesmiths, Fletching, Artillery
- **Woodworkers: **Architects, Carpenters, Cartwrights, Foresters, Loggers

## **Major Guild **Descriptions**, Guildmasters, Minor Guilds**

### **Armorersmiths**

GM: Astrid Eriksdottir (F) (Katoites)

Minor Guilds: Automaton, Machinist

The Armorersmith Guild stands for its craftsmanship, renowned across Leandan for their mastery of scale, chain, split, and plate armor. Their skill in forging protective gear is unmatched, providing warriors with armor that combines both durability and elegance. However, their expertise extends beyond traditional armaments.

Within the hallowed halls of the guild, they nurture the burgeoning field of Automatons, delving into the realm of mechanical marvels. Here, the Minor Guild Houses of Machinists and Automation find their home, where they collaborate to push the boundaries of innovation. Automation, the art of crafting and refining robotics, is a discipline embraced and honed within the guild's walls, driving progress and ushering in a new era of technological advancement.

### **Brewers**

GM: Rajesh Singh (M) (Thrani)

Minor Guilds: Distillers, Vitners

Found in the idyllic island scenery of Corai lies the Grand Guild of Brewers, a testament to the artistry and ingenuity of its members. Here, amid verdant landscapes and gentle sea breezes, master brewers ply their trade, harnessing the island's unique yeasts and tinctures to craft a cornucopia of libations. From crisp ales to robust stouts, delicate wines to fiery spirits, the Brewer's Guild caters to every palate within the Leandan League. As a beacon of craftsmanship and creativity, the Brewer's Guild continues to enchant and delight connoisseurs far and wide, its offerings a testament to the rich tapestry of flavors found within the realm.

### **Culinarian**

GM: Antonio Fernandez (M) (Paltrist)

Minor Guilds: Bakers, Butchers

The Guild of Culinarians stands as the vanguard of culinary excellence, tirelessly pushing the boundaries of gastronomic innovation across the nation. From the comforting warmth of humble hearth soups to the refined intricacies of dishes served to the esteemed Three Chancellors, the Culinarians infuse every meal with passion and expertise. While every kitchen boasts a cook, those aspiring to elevate their culinary prowess flock to the Guild of Culinarians.

Within its hallowed halls, apprentices learn the artistry of flavor, texture, and presentation, honing their skills under the guidance of master chefs. Here, the minor guilds of Baking and Butchers find their homes as well, complementing the culinary journey with the essential crafts of creating delectable bread and procuring the finest cuts of meat. As guardians of culinary tradition and pioneers of gastronomic evolution, the Guild of Culinarians ensures that every meal is a masterpiece worthy of celebration.

### **Farmers - Member of Small Council**

GM: Marta Mendoza (F) (Paltrist)

Minor Guilds: Husbandary

The Farmer's Guild stands as the guardians of Leandan's agricultural abundance, laboring tirelessly to cultivate the rich soil that sustains the nation. With skilled hands and unwavering dedication, they till the earth's fertile depths, coaxing forth an abundance of fruits and vegetables that grace every table.

Renowned for their mastery over the land, they possess a keen ability to safeguard their crops from illness, ensuring both quality and quantity in every harvest. But their expertise extends beyond the fields, as they also oversee the Minor Guild of Husbandry, meticulously breeding livestock to produce only the finest animals, be they destined for the dusty roads or the meal table. With a commitment to excellence and a deep respect for the land, the Farmer's Guild remains a cornerstone of Leandan's prosperity, ensuring that no table goes unfed and no stomach remains empty.

### **Glasssmith**

GM: Farid Al-Maghribi (M) (Paltrist)

Minor Guilds: Glassware, Lensers

The Glasssmith's Guild stands as the master craftsmen of Leandan, wielding the transformative power of fire and sand to shape ordinary materials into exquisite works of art. With skilled hands and meticulous precision, they take coarse white sands and imbue them with beauty and utility, producing fine panes of glass that adorn windows and illuminate interiors with natural light.

But their artistry doesn't end there; within their ranks, the minor guild of Lensers elevates their craft to new heights. These skilled artisans refine their glass to create specialized lenses for a variety of purposes, from the spyglasses used by ship captains to the delicate scopes employed by Physicians. With a commitment to perfection and an unwavering dedication to their craft, the Glasssmith's Guild ensures that Leandan's vision is always crystal clear, both literally and metaphorically.

### **Harmony**

GM: Robin Van der Heijden (F) (Welvart)

Minor Guilds: Undertaker, Guide, Arbiter, Assassins

The Guild of Harmony operates with a facade of compassion and support, yet beneath its benevolent exterior lies a clandestine arm known only to a select few. Comprised of three overt divisions and one covert sect, the guild orchestrates the delicate balance between life and death.

Undertakers, revered for their solemnity and skill, reverently prepare the departed for their Sitting—a final tribute akin to a Wake—before facilitating their dignified transition to cremation. Guides, adept therapists, guide grieving families through the tumultuous seas of loss, serving as liaisons between bereaved kin and the enigmatic Arbiter. Tasked with deciphering the labyrinthine laws surrounding estates, the Arbiter ensures the equitable distribution of assets while safeguarding the interests of both family and state. Yet, hidden amidst this facade of solace and legality, lurk the Assassins, silent arbiters of a more enigmatic decree.

Some of us pass peacefully. Some of us should simply pass. For the harmony of the whole.

### **Jewelers - Member of Small Council**

GM: Amélie Picard (F) (Quintill)

Minor Guilds: Gemist, Gem Cutter

At the pinnacle of wealth and influence within the Leandan League stands the Jewelers Guild, a formidable force wielding the key to the nation's technology and prosperity: the coveted Crystals of Leandan. With unparalleled expertise, they possess the knowledge to harness the inherent powers of these precious gems, carving them into useful forms and unlocking their latent energies.

Within their ranks, the Gemists meticulously grade and evaluate each crystal, ensuring only the finest specimens are utilized for their intended purposes. Alongside them, the skilled Gem Cutters ply their trade, shaping the crystals into exquisite creations tailored to meet both industrial demands and personal indulgences.

Hand in hand with the Miner's Guild, they extract these valuable resources from the earth, forging a path of prosperity and innovation for the entire nation. As guardians of Leandan's most coveted treasures, the Jewelers Guild stands as a beacon of wealth, power, and technological advancement, shaping the destiny of the land and its people.

### **Leatherworkers**

GM: Jin Wei (M) (Thao)

Minor Guilds: Tanners

The Leatherworker's Guild stands as the cornerstone of Leandan's craftsmanship, supplying the essential material for endeavors ranging from the most humble to the most noble. With expert skill and dedication, they transform raw hides into supple leather, crafting a myriad of goods that serve both practical and luxurious purposes.

Within their guild halls, the minor guild of Tanners works tirelessly to prepare and refine the hides, ensuring they are of the highest quality before being fashioned into finished products. From sturdy boots and durable saddles to elegant bags and fine garments, the Leatherworker's Guild supplies the diverse needs of the community. Whether for personal use or the service of others, their creations exemplify the timeless artistry and dedication that define Leandan's esteemed guilds.

### **Parnassians**

GM: Jesper Hagen

Minor Guilds: Actors, Painting, Musicians, Sculpting, Songbirds, Storytellers

The Parnassian Guild of Leandan stands as a vibrant beacon of artistic expression, encompassing a diverse array of talents including actors, musicians, singers, and writers and poets of every ilk. These creative virtuosos are the driving force behind the cultural tapestry of the realm, igniting the flames of inspiration in all corners of society. From taverns resonating with melodies to stages adorned with captivating performances, and from shelves adorned with fantastical tales to galleries showcasing exquisite works of art, the Parnassian Guild leaves an indelible mark on the collective imagination of Leandan. Within its esteemed ranks, specialized artisans thrive in various disciplines, including Actors who bring characters to life, Painters who capture beauty on canvas, Musicians who evoke emotions through melody, Sculptors who shape raw materials into masterpieces, Songbirds who enchant audiences with their voices, and Storytellers who weave captivating narratives that transport listeners to distant realms. Together, the Parnassian Guild fosters a rich cultural landscape, celebrating the boundless creativity and artistic prowess of Leandan's inhabitants.

### **Physicians **

GM: Bjorn Einarsson (M) (Katoites)

Minor Guilds: Apothecaries, Boffins, Midwives, Veterinarians

The Physicians Guild stands as a beacon of healing within Leandan society, dedicated to the noble art of restoring health and vitality to those in need. Physicians possess the knowledge and skill to diagnose and treat a myriad of ailments.

While pharmacologists ply their trade in the Apothecaries, crafting remedies and potions, Physicians specialize in the intricate understanding of the human body and its workings. Within their ranks, one can find the learned Boffins, adept chemists who contribute their expertise to the guild's vast repository of medical knowledge. In their dedication to holistic care, they embrace the indispensable role of Midwives, guiding new life into the world with tenderness and expertise.

Moreover, the Physicians Guild also encompasses the Minor Guild of Veterinarians, ensuring that their healing touch extends not only to humans but also to the beloved animals that share our world.

### **Scribes**

GM: Elin Magnusdottir (F) (Katoites)

Minor Guilds: Bookbinders, Cartographers, Illuminators, Public Servants

The Grand Guild of Scribes stands as a citadel of knowledge and education within the realm of Leandan, its members revered as both keepers of wisdom and mentors to the young. Tasked with preserving and beautifying the written word for posterity, Scribes dedicate themselves to the art of transcription, ensuring that the annals of history are recorded with meticulous precision.

Yet their duties extend beyond mere transcription, for Scribes also serve as educators, imparting the invaluable skills of reading, writing, and oration to the youth of the land. Within the hallowed halls of the Scribes' Guild, one may find not only scholars and teachers, but also the skilled artisans of the Minor Guilds of Bookbinders, Cartographers, and Illuminators, each contributing to the grand tapestry of knowledge.

While the Minor Guild House of Public Servitude nominally falls under the auspices of the Scribes, its practitioners are dispersed throughout the realm, their dedication to public service ingrained in the very fabric of Leandan society. Nonetheless, aspiring public servants may seek guidance and instruction at the local guilds of the Scribes, where they can hone their skills and prepare for a life of service to their fellow citizens.

### **Miners**

GM: Cornelis van der Zwaan (M) (Welvart)

Minor Guilds: Masons, Stonecutters

The Leandan Miners represent the backbone of the nation, tirelessly extracting the coveted Crystals from the depths of the earth. As the workhorses of the realm, they embody steadfastness and resilience, their dedication unwavering as they toil in the darkness of the mines.

Often characterized by their soft-spoken demeanor, mirroring that of their leader, the miners exemplify humility and diligence in their craft. Alongside them, Masons and Stonecutters play integral roles within the mining industry. While both professions involve working with stone, Masons focus on building structures, utilizing stone, bricks, and other materials to construct walls, buildings, and other architectural features. On the other hand, Stonecutters specialize in shaping and carving stone into specific shapes and designs, often for decorative or ornamental purposes.

Together, Miners, Masons, and Stonecutters collaborate seamlessly with multiple industries, ensuring that Leandan receives nothing but the finest craftsmanship and resources. Their collective efforts contribute significantly to the prosperity and advancement of the nation.

### **Shipwrights - Member of Small Council**

GM: Henri Duval (M) (Quintill)

Minor Guilds: Airships, Fishing, Navigators, Netmakers, Sailmakers, Whalers

The Shipwrights of Leandan stand as a formidable force, wielding influence over maritime trade and industry throughout the realm. At the forefront of commerce and transportation, they command the seas with their expertly crafted vessels, facilitating the exchange of goods and resources with distant shores. Spearheading trade negotiations and overseeing the construction of mighty ships, the Shipwrights Guild plays a pivotal role in Leandan's economic prosperity and global outreach.

However, amidst their dominance in traditional maritime affairs, tensions simmer with their minor guild, the Airships. While the Shipwrights control the seas, the Airships take flight, seeking independence from their maritime counterparts. Despite their differing designs and purposes, the Shipwrights maintain a firm grip on the Airships, reluctant to relinquish their authority. It creates an overwhelming tension at all levels of guildship.

Within the guild's expansive domain, various trades find their home, including Fishing, Navigators, Netmakers, Sailmakers, and Whalers. Each profession contributes to the guild's collective prowess, ensuring that the Shipwrights Guild remains a cornerstone of Leandan's maritime supremacy.

### **Smiths**

GM: Elise Dubois (F) (Quintill)

Minor Guilds: Finesmiths, Locksmiths

While the Armorsmiths and Weaponsmiths have branched off to specialize in their respective fields, the Smiths of the Leandan League remain the versatile artisans, adept at shaping metal into a myriad of forms, all everyday but never commonplace. From crafting intricate silver platters to fashioning lush door knobs, their skill knows no bounds. Whether it's a delicate golden inlay or robust locks to secure treasured possessions, the Smiths pride themselves on their meticulous workmanship. Within their guild, the minor branches of Finesmiths and Locksmiths further hone their craft, focusing on the finer details of metalwork and the art of locksmithing respectively. Together, the Leandan Smiths continue to uphold the tradition of excellence in metal craftsmanship, ensuring that every piece they forge is a testament to their skill and dedication.

### **Tailors**

GM: Anneliese van der Berg (F) (Welvart)

Minor Guild: Cobblers

The Tailors Leandan stands as a beacon of fashion and style, collaborating closely with their sister guild, the Weavers, to adorn the citizens of Leandan in garments of unparalleled elegance. From the opulent attire of the elite to the everyday wear of the common folk, the Tailors strive to elevate the fashion sensibilities of all. With an eye for detail and a passion for innovation, they craft garments that are a harmonious blend of fabric, texture, color, and contrast, reflecting the diverse tastes and personalities of their clientele. In their pursuit of sartorial excellence, the Tailors work hand in hand with their minor guilds, the Cobblers, ensuring that from head to toe, every individual is impeccably dressed and ready to make a statement. With their commitment to creativity and craftsmanship, the Tailors Guild continues to shape the aesthetic landscape of Leandan, leaving an indelible mark on its fashion scene.

### **Weavers**

GM: Kavita Patel (F) (Thrani)

Minor Guild: Dyers

The Weavers Guild of Leandan serves as the heart and soul of the nation's textile industry, breathing life into the very fabric of Leandan society. Collaborating closely with their sister guild, the Tailors, they transform raw fibers into exquisite works of art that adorn the citizens of Leandan. With a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for cultural diversity, the Weavers craft intricate patterns and prints that celebrate the vibrant tapestry of cultures found within the realm. Their craftsmanship knows no bounds, ranging from intimate handloom creations to the grandeur of water-powered mills that dot the landscape of larger cities. Alongside their masterpieces, the Weavers also oversee the work of their minor guild, the Dyers Guild, ensuring that every thread is imbued with rich and vibrant hues that evoke the essence of Leandan's beauty and splendor. Through their dedication to their craft, the Weavers Guild continues to weave together the threads of tradition and innovation, leaving an indelible mark on the fabric of Leandan society.

### **Weaponsmiths**

GM: Eero Virtanen (F) (Katoites)

Minor Guilds: Bladesmiths, Fletching, Artillery

The Weaponsmiths of the Leandan League have emerged as stalwart artisans in their own right, having evolved from their origins within the Armorers Guild to establish a distinguished presence of their own. These skilled craftsmen and women are entrusted with the vital task of arming the noble defenders of Leandan. Collaborating closely with the Miners Guild, they meticulously procure the metals and minerals essential for their craft. From grand swords that gleam with righteous purpose to formidable ballistae that defend the realm's borders, Weaponsmiths fashion an array of armaments that cater to the diverse needs of the League's populace. Within their ranks reside specialized artisans known as Bladesmiths, who master the forging of bladed weapons, Fletchers skilled in crafting projectiles, and Artillery experts versed in the construction of siege engines. Together, the Weaponsmiths uphold a legacy of excellence in armament production, ensuring that the defenders of Leandan are equipped to face any challenge that may arise.

### **Woodworkers**

GM: Pieter van der Waal

Minor Guilds: Architects, Carpenters, Cartwrights, Foresters, Loggers

The Woodworker's Guild of Leandan stands as a bastion of craftsmanship and stewardship, seamlessly blending the art of woodworking with the preservation of the League's lush forests. With a deep respect for nature's bounty, they ethically harvest the finest woods from the forested realms, ensuring the sustainability of their craft for generations to come. Within their guild halls, master craftsmen transform these raw materials into both the everyday necessities and the extraordinary marvels that adorn Leandan's homes and buildings. Moreover, the Woodworker's Guild serves as a haven for the Architects of Leandan, visionaries who design and construct the awe-inspiring structures that grace the landscape. Comprising minor guilds such as Architects, Carpenters, Cartwrights, Foresters, and Loggers, the Woodworker's Guild is not only a testament to skilled artisanship but also a champion of environmental stewardship and architectural innovation.
