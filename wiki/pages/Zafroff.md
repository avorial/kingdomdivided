# History:

In the annals of ancient legends, the Zafroff people have a complex and intriguing history that weaves together their roots in a society influenced by the Worm God known as Voraxia. Originating as a barbarian people known for their raiding and pillaging ways, their paths converged with the followers of the Star in the lands of Fygor.

Upon encountering the followers of the Star, a profound transformation occurred within the Zafroff. Some members of their tribe found solace and purpose for the first time, igniting a schism between those who wished to preserve their old ways and those who embraced the teachings of the Star. In a civil war that ensued, the faction aligned with the Star emerged victorious but faced ongoing scrutiny and skepticism for their past actions.

Motivated to prove their worthiness and transcend the shadows of their history, the Zafroff dedicated themselves to achieving excellence beyond expectations. This collective drive propelled them to push the boundaries of their abilities, striving to become invaluable assets in the eyes of others, seeking recognition and acceptance as equals.
