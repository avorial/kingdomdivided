=History=

Windbreak started as a key port on the west for centuries, as it was then chosen as the site to start the Grand Canal it's populatary rose. For all of the planing done by House Sedi this was a poor place to start, the geopolitics made it a perfect target for pirates able to supply off of the Saffron Isle. In just a few generations it was known as the pirate capital of the Bell Lands. Every time House Henge would send troops, the pirates would just set sail for a month or two and then return. House Ramirez formally took the city with the founding of the Pirate Kings Alliance of House Ramirez & House Wycliff.

As time past and the success of the Pirate Kings, Ennobled them Windbreak became the capital of the domain of the east then known as Silverridge. With now the naval power supporting the town it prospered. While never the beauty of some of the larger cities on the continent, It is a mercantile power that can not be ignored.

==Religious Sites==

There are a few bellringer churches and there is also a small chapel in Windbreak Keep. The hills north of the city hold an Ruins of Windbreak|old tower that is an active site for religious services of the Telung Gods.

The highest ranking Bellringer is Blessed Philip Nokum, While the leader of the Telung Gods cults is currently unknown.

==Crime==

Crime in the city is rampant, but they make a point to focus on areas away from the keep. The most common crimes are protection, gambling, and loan sharks. The leader of the criminal element is Erick Sanderson.
