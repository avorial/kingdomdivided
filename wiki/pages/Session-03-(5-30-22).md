# Events

Just outside of Deerholt the party sends their Enisen members to go and do a reconnaissance. During that they kill 2 bandits and see a Saffron Society Priest cast a spell. They run back to the party, and the party decides to attack now, hoping the spells can not be cast constantly.

As they sneak in they get to a bar, where they meet a Bellringer Priest (Anya Mosley), who tells them that there is a warrior in the town but his ax was taken away and the players have the opportunity to deliver it to him. They kill a few more bandits before they get to O’rih Silverridge house where they meet his son (Cormac Silverridge), the warrior.

They gradually learn that O’rih Silverridge is a man of legend in the House Silverridge background, and he gives them a plan to attack the priest when he is asleep the party burn his house but he is rescued by a dark stranger who flees with him. The party decides to return to Windbreak, saying goodbye to the Spring Oath Brothers (Gregory Yabarra, Sir Luis Covinggard and Zain Forester) Amund Henge as they go.

As they leave they decide to name Anya Mosley a knight and Mayor of Deerholt
