# Events

Caden Mattix as a child is a squire to Adan Mcneill. They go though a Mullrose on their way to Wickham. On the trip Caden Mattix learns his master killed a noble of House Covinggard using information he should not have had. House Covinggard has wanted him dead since then. After meeting all the nobles and making friends with a group of House Graisti knights of House Lombardo before the tourney starts.  Adan Mcneill does well the first 2 matches beating the House Covinggard knights who wanted him dead, but the final match is vs Leon Blackwood. With his skill he kills Adan Mcneill in one bout, becoming the nemesis of Caden Mattix. Caden Mattix Leaves taking the horse he has to this day.
