# Cultures

Cultures of Humans found in Kingdom Divided.

- Avestan  -- A dusky skinned people who are renowned to be the slavers of Kadal
- Dannu -- An old culture who are blessed by the star gods, most commonly living in Fygor
- Esten -- The people of the trees, an albino people who have lost their great kingdom after the first bellringer crusade
- [[Fedor|Fedor]]-- A red haired people who are believed to come from an island called “the land of fire and flowers” they made their way to the bellands.
- [[Helior|Helior]]-- A giant race who stand commonly 7 feet tall, they have clan societies, and have some of the deepest connections to the old world.
- [[Katoites|Katoites]]-- A race of builders who's crafts are desired around the world.
- Koralia -- The people of the stones, the mirror of the Esten they are fewer in number but are renowned for their resilience.
- [[Paltris|Paltris]]-- A demined people who succeed when trials are hard, but have trouble dealing with leaders who have not proven their worth.
- [[Quintill|Quintill]] -- A people who are refined and gracious, they are seen as natural leaders.
- [[Saltblood|Saltblood]]-- A people who are renowned for their seafaring traditions, and island cultures.
- [[Thao|Thao]]-- Masters of their destiny, they have the fates on their side when conflicts arise.
- [[Thrani|Thrani]]-- A golden skinned people who have wisdom of previous ages they can tap in to.
- [[Welvart|Welvart]]-- Originally oppressed by the Avestan, they can speak to the heart of men, and thus have all languages at their fingertips.
- [[Zafroff|Zafroff]]-- A driven people who feel perfection is the only goal worth striving for.

# Races

Races, other than human.

- The “Maw” People -- there are untold races in the caverns of the heartstone, that come up and attack the land. They are normally thought to be old nightmares, or legends said by grandmothers to make children behave.
