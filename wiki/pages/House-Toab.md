## [¶](#house-toab) **House Toab**

**Title:** The Steady Hand  
**Former Title:** Ai Hoku (Held during the Great War of Unification)  
**Location:** Ivory Basin, Southern Expanse  
**Faith:** Dual followers of the Worm Gods and the Gods of the Heart  
**Role:** Statesmanship, policy, and internal development

### [¶](#overview) **Overview**

House Toab is old, steady, and wise—known for maintaining stability through governance rather than might. Though they once held the Ai Hoku title during the War of Unification, they gracefully ceded power when House Abbyean rose through martial triumph. They now focus on economic oversight, agricultural reform, and philosophical discourse.

Toab’s influence is strongest within the Mikado court, where they are considered a moral compass and source of tradition. The young and ambitious often see them as obsolete, but those who understand the deeper currents of power in the Empire know: House Toab rarely speaks unless it truly matters.

### [¶](#leadership) **Leadership**

**Lord Varen Toab**  
A master orator and political sage, Lord Varen has served as Royal Vizier for over two decades. Known for his soft voice and impossibly sharp wit, he rarely shows emotion in court. He is said to have drafted over half of the current Empire’s economic laws.

**High Lady Evara Toab**  
A renowned philosopher and architect of the public education system in the southern provinces. Her teachings on “Civic Harmony in a Fractured Age” are widely studied by noble youths.

### [¶](#key-members) **Key Members**

**Seneschal Briem Toab**  
An aging economist and House strategist who oversees all internal trade routes. He once brokered peace between five feuding provinces without raising a sword.

**Young Lord Oren Toab**  
Varen’s grandson and a rising political star. Known for speeches that stir the youth and ideas that challenge the old order. He may be a spark for future reform—or rebellion.
