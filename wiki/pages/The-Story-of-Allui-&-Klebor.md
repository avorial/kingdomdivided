# Allui & Klebor

The founder of the Esten Ancestry is accredited to Allui. Allui was not what we would consider human, she was a living tree. Trees then would walk and talk, finding who they loved. When that love was found they would plant themselves near their dearest and grow old together.

Allui, could not find common ground with her other trees. She tried over and over, but a match was never made. Until she met Klebor. Kelbor was not a tree but a living boulder. At first it was their alien natures that attracted them, but as time went they found they were true soulmates. Not knowing how to do a marriage of rock and tree, they fumbled around one another as many a young couple does. Each being changed in the experience.

Allui and Klebor, love grew as did her belly. Until one day they came upon the a lake. Jumping in it for trying a new experience, as they got father and farther from the shore, Kelbor was being pulled down, as Allui was floating. They held as tight as they could, but the water was stronger then both of them. Allui saw Klebor sink into the darkness. She made it to the shore heartbroken. She cried for days and days. When the weather started to turn, she decided that she would return to her forests, and have their child there.

But as soon as she entered the grove she realized she was now a stranger. Her bark was shaved down by her tumblings with Klebor, white as snow her flesh was. Her eyes were red from all the tears, all hoping she could see Klebor in the dark waters. The trees still took her in, and sheltered her from the rains. On the first day of spring she gave birth to a boy and a girl the first Enisen.
