# Synopsis

**Arrival of the Hussars:**

- The group led by Litlia Mookjai arrives at night in a well-patrolled area, facilitating faster travel.

**Diplomatic Mission:**

- Eloyse questions Casimir about their journey through Almirin territory. Casimir explains that he's supporting Siobhan and others in their diplomatic endeavors.

**Jungle Survival Tips:**

- Litlia provides Peter with a lesson on jungle survival, covering topics like clothing, armor, bug glow sticks, and traveling off-road.

**Imperial Messenger's Death:**

- Litlia reveals that an imperial messenger was found dead on the road, sparking curiosity among the group members, especially Julien.

**Food and Survival Queries:**

- Siobhan seeks advice from Litlia regarding Tanya Bennett's suggestions on diet. Litlia mentions the Pituke, bugs that lay their eggs in humans.

**Armor Discussion:**

- Litlia advises Casimir on outfits and equipment, mentioning the potential rust on his armor and suggesting a leather type of armor.

**Investigation and Arrest Warrant:**

- Julien investigates the dead imperial messenger, finding an arrest warrant for Louis Covinggard. They decide to send the information back to the capital to delay spreading the news.

**Camp Setup and Jungle Encounter:**

- The group sets up camp outside the jungle and encounters Panther Gators. They face a larger, menacing creature, prompting them to seek refuge on a hill.

**Discovery of Ancient **Esten**City:**

- Exploring the hill, they discover an ancient Esten city with obsidian veins. Casimir shares his experiences with the cursed Soul Glass.

**Symbolic Gemstones and Anthem:**

- Siobhan finds a marker of gemstones, and when Lyla transforms into a full Esten, the symbols harmonize like a national anthem.

**Night Exploration and Blue People:**

- Litlia and the boys sneak into a different area of the city at night, encountering blue people with pendants on their foreheads.

**Confrontation and Brass Sphere:**

- Julien attacks a blue creature, leading to a confrontation with a Temple Guard. They obtain a brass sphere, which turns out to be a ley line map.

**Sovereign's Darkness:**

- Sovereign's encounter with a mirror causes his darkness to consume him. Julien kills the Temple Guard, and they take a brass dial with them.

**Teleportation Gem and Ley Line Map:**

- Sovereign investigates the Temple Guard's forehead, finding a teleportation gem. The brass sphere reveals ley lines on the planet, potentially a map of great significance.
