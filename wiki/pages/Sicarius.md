## [¶](#history) History

The Sicarius are is an assassins guild dealing mostly with the islands surrounding the Bell Lands and Mikado Empire. They have 3 levels of initiation.

The first is the called the newborn.

*The "newborns" are those who have just been sent on their first missions traditionally these missions are far away from their homelands. As many do not return because they are either killed or leave the cult little is lost.

The second it called the target.

*When they have killed enough people to gain a reputation and have proven loyalty to the cult, they are given an apprentice to train in to becoming the newborn. They are also introduced in the some of the mysteries of the cult, and deeper motivations.

The third is called the dead.

*The are those who have given years to the cult, many who have been wounded or aged out of the day to day work of killing. They work for the negotiations on targets and know the true secrets of what they serve.
