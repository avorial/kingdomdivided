# Events

## Hungry Wolves

The party gives an update to Carrick Silverridge as they hear noise outside. They check outside and it's Diego Valls a member of the troops that worked under Casimir Ramirez. File:Deigo Valls.jpg|right|thumb He meets the group, and he tells them that the Hungry Wolves are going to join House Silverridge troops. Casimir Ramirez also asks them to prep to back him up in calling out Sir Timoté Durand for a duel.

## Letter of Intent

Casimir Ramirez gets a note from Baroness Penelope Durand that she will marry him if he wins a duel vs Timoté Durand. But she wants a guarantee that House Silverridge will kill Timoté Durand even if he wins. The cadre agree they will take it to Duke Connor Silverridge who signs it while he is in a meeting with Duchess Olivia Pearstone.

## The Report Arrives

They all meet back up hearing talking in the next room they go and see Cormac Silverridge File:Cormac Silverridge.jpg|thumb|right talking to Connor Silverridge. He says that he has joined the Knights of House Rhelrya and that he has Carrick Silverridge to thank for that.

He also gives a note, from Windbreak that says Sir Hans Rhienheart rebuked an attack by Cynog Derth and a group of mercenaries. And they are being held under guard. The key issue is Cynog Derth was sent by Connor Silverridge to take the city. The letter seems to imply though that Sir Hans Rhienheart's secret is not publicly known and he still openly supports House Silverridge.

People give updates on what happened with the day, and Connor Silverridge gives 300 silver to Carrick Silverridge to get a ring for his bride.
