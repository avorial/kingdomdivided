House Baiersbruck is a relatively new house that has made a name for itself in the courts of Windbreak. They started as beekeepers and used their honey to make mead, which became quite popular in the region. With their newfound wealth, they invested in various industries and quickly became an industrial power in the region. They even gained a few knighthoods in the prestigious Ashen Knights. House Baiersbruck’s rise to power has not gone unnoticed.

They are an influential force in the courts of Windbreak and have close ties with House Montimer. They control the lands between the Montimer and Silverridge, two powerful houses that have been in conflict for decades. House Baiersbruck tries to stay on the positive side of both houses to ensure that they lose the least if war does break out.

Despite their success, House Baiersbruck has faced challenges in getting their children into the halls of power. They have been unable to make that leap, but they are determined to continue growing their influence in the courts of Windbreak. Their ambition and determination have made them a force to be reckoned with, and their legacy is only expected to grow in the years to come
