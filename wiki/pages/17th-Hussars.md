The 17th Hussars, is a primary unit of the Montimerian knights, a member of the order of Ashen Knights.

Rituals and Traditions.

1. Every Morning they sing a song, this is a penance for attacking a Bellringer Nunnery.
2. At Regimental Dinners they “drink” a cup of ashes, in memory of the founder of Montmier
3. The “honor guard” before anyone can drink has to walk a line on top of the table, this is a “proof” that he has not been drinking
4. They toast “the rose of Montmier” duchess Lucy every night at 12
