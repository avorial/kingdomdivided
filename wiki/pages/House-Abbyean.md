## [¶](#house-abbyean) **House Abbyean**

**Title:** The Shield of the Empire  
**Current Title:** Ai Hoku (Star Eater – Supreme Military Commander)  
**Location:** Fortress-City of Daibara, Eastern Reach  
**Faith:** Nominally Worm Gods, also practices battlefield ancestor rites  
**Role:** Military leadership and frontline defense

### [¶](#overview) **Overview**

House Abbyean rose to prominence during the recent resurgence of conflict with the Star Gods and their crusading proxies. Possessing a proud military heritage, they are the sword and shield of the Mikado Empire, with every member trained in the art of war and strategy from youth. Their fortress-home Daibara sits closest to the belligerent Bellringer Crusaders and has become a living bastion of war.

They are respected but feared—ruthless in battle and often aloof in diplomacy. Other noble houses both envy and resent their current status as Ai Hoku, a role that gives them near-unchecked power in military affairs.

### [¶](#leadership) **Leadership**

**General Ai Hoku Kazar Abbyean**  
A weathered warrior in his 50s, Kazar commands the armies of the Empire with unmatched tenacity. Scarred from dozens of campaigns, he wears war-armor adorned with ancient weapons of defeated crusader commanders. Though stern and unbending, Kazar is considered a military genius and has never lost a siege. Some say he would make a better Emperor than general, but he refuses any title beyond Ai Hoku.

**Lady Suria Abbyean**  
Kazar’s daughter and heir apparent. A brilliant tactician and fierce duelist, she commands a division of elite sky-cavalry known as the Fire Larks. Rumored to have a rivalry with Prince Shenren of House Mikado.

### [¶](#key-members) **Key Members**

**Commander Thran Abbyean**  
Kazar’s younger brother and the Empire’s Master of Siege. Inventor of the smoke-dragon engines now used across all fronts. Charismatic but politically dangerous.

**Sister-Regent Enwei Abbyean**  
A field priestess and medium who blesses troops before battles. Though considered eccentric, her rituals are often cited as the reason for House Abbyean’s continued victories.
