# SE: The Age of the Sedi Dynasty

*SE is the name of the age in the Bell Lands It's described as the Sedi Epoch. For ease of chronicling it's given in reverse dates like BCE in our world. The major confusion is it goes back somewhat farther than the actual rise of [[House Sedi]]. Most Learned records go back only to the Great Conquest. This occurred because of two key reasons. First the invaders of the Lands of Earth and Water destroyed many of the Noble Esten libraries. Secondly few of the early generations of crusaders and their families knew more of the histories deeper than a generation.*

## 157 SE

Since the rise of House Sedi to the Imperial seat, the cause of the Church of the Bellringers has split. The simple faith of the common man and noble, was a very orthodox teaching of the Light of the Bellringer. Those that were more learned or seekers felt called to another teaching, a teaching known as the Peals. The Peals felt that the Bell Ringer was not the stars above, but the darkness between them as a man covered by a bell is in darkness so is the true message of the Bellringer. The scisim reached a new level as the Grand Hierophant declared all Pellists heretics and that any who gave them succor would be burned alive and the Second Great Bellringer Crusade started.

## 141 SE

Grand Hierophant Watson declares that Montrose Sedi, the Heir of the House Sedi was to be burned. There was no doubt he was a Pellist, but still his father tried to convince the Grand Hierophant to be lenient, but as heresy was growing in the country an example had to be made. As he was to be taken to the pyre a group of Pellist monks ambushed the guards and fled Oldwyn. The time for silence was over, Brother against brother, house against house, the war flared up on both sides of the Bell Lands.

## 148 SE

Montrose found by a group of bellringers in a barn, the barn is burned down as he becomes a myatar to the Pellists. In this act the Pellists start taking land, primarily in the North west, finding support in the Covernguard and Rivers Courts.

## 110 SE

The last formal Pellist army in destroyed on the feild after that it is just ambush raids and the church burning those they feel were supporters.

## 98 SE

As Grand Hierophant Watson is turning ill, in his 90th year he calls an end to the Crusade, feeling that it has been turned in to a political tool.

# RE: The Age of the Rivers Empire

## 0 SE

The Fall of the Sedi Dynasty: House Sedi had a army that was not filled with their own people. House Rivers had infiltrated their own armies with troops that were not loyal. Even the secret police of House Sedi was trained in rivers domain, they were known as Shadow Guard people who could teleport through shadows. Fleeing Oldwyn the royal army was attacked by their own troops. Pushing south they had to give up all their wealth to turn some of the mercenaries to their side. As they made it to Bradninch, House Talmart stood with their archers and knights. While they would not give the final blow. For the previous crimes of House Sedi destroying the other Esten houses they would not be allowed to pass.  Turning around the weight of the House Covenguard and House Rivers Troops had pushed them by the river at Orholt. House Sedi used ancient magics to call on the elements to push the troops back, and where causing loses on the rebels side. Duke Matthias Rivers marches out to parlay with the Emperor Rustam Sedi it seems that after 3 hours a deal was struck for peace.  As Emperor Rustam Sedi rides away, a lone archer from the House Rivers toops, shoots an arrow in the head of Rustam Sedi killing him instantly. The archer is crucified, but the House Sedi troops have nothing left to fight for. In exchange for bending the knee, the troops are allowed to scatter.
