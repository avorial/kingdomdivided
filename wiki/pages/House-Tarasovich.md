# History

When House Tarasovich was forced into hiding, adopting the name of their lesser-known cadet branch, House Tarasovich, they knew that secrecy and careful maneuvering would be crucial to their survival. Under their new identity, they operated discreetly, making use of their hidden wealth and connections to gradually regain power and influence in the shadows. Their only hubris keeping the “Ides" moniker showing who currently is the Sedi Emperor.

The mastermind behind their hidden stability was Lord Alistair Tarasovich, a cunning and charismatic leader. Alistair understood the importance of alliances and strategic partnerships. He began forging connections with other noble houses, particularly House Covenguard and House Polansky, who had their own grievances against House Rivers. Together, they established the "Coalition of Shadows," a network of like-minded houses committed to reclaiming their lost positions and challenging the authority of House Rivers.

To safeguard their interests and maintain their hidden status, House Tarasovich relied on the elite warriors known as the Shadow Guard. These skilled individuals, handpicked by Lord Alistair himself from the remaining members of the royal guard, possessed the ability to teleport through shadows, thanks to the lingering shadow magic inherited from Ethan Sedi. However, this power came with a curse, corrupting those who wielded it and necessitating the utmost secrecy, as the use of such magic was outlawed by the Rivers Emperors.

One of the significant turning points for House Tarasovich came when Lord Alistair embraced the teachings of the Pellist Heresy of the Bellringers. Recognizing the potential of this heretical movement to challenge the established order and aid in their quest for influence, House Tarasovich became strong supporters of the heresy. They provided financial support and forged alliances with influential individuals within the movement, allowing them to gain trust and exert their influence behind the scenes.

Throughout the years of hiding, House Tarasovich faced numerous challenges and setbacks. They encountered spies and informants seeking to expose their true identity, forcing them to constantly adapt and employ deceptive tactics. However, their resilience and resourcefulness allowed them to weather these storms and continue their gradual rise.

Within the Coalition of Shadows, there were several key figures who played pivotal roles in House Tarasovich's clandestine operations. Lady Isabella Covenguard, a skilled diplomat and strategist, became a trusted ally and confidante of Lord Alistair. Her ability to navigate the treacherous political landscape and her knowledge of covert operations were invaluable assets to House Tarasovich.

Another important character was Lord Dominik Polansky, the head of House Polansky, known for his unwavering loyalty and fierce determination. Lord Dominik provided crucial military support, training a formidable force of loyal soldiers who would aid House Tarasovich in their future endeavors.

Over time, House Tarasovich gradually regained their lost influence. They orchestrated intricate schemes and political maneuvers, manipulating rival houses and exploiting divisions within the empire. Through careful manipulation of the banking system in the Bell Lands, they funneled wealth into their coffers, solidifying their financial power and influence.

However, their rise did not go unnoticed. House Rivers, always vigilant to potential threats, attempted to uncover the truth behind the Coalition of Shadows. They launched investigations, purged suspected members, and intensified their efforts to maintain control. House Tarasovich and their allies had to stay one step ahead, employing secrecy, misdirection, and manipulation to protect themselves and their hidden activities.

As the generations passed, leadership of House Tarasovich transitioned from Lord Alistair to his descendants. Each successive leader carried the weight of their family's legacy, constantly strategizing and scheming to expand their influence while avoiding detection. The challenges they faced were many, but their determination to reclaim their lost empire and emerge from the shadows fueled their relentless pursuit of power.

House Tarasovich's journey of hiding, scheming, and regaining influence spanned two centuries. Their story weaves through the tapestry of the empire's history, filled with hidden alliances, secret operations, and the indomitable spirit of a house determined to reclaim its rightful place in the realm.

## The Time of Boris Tarasovich

Boris Tarasovich was a scion of House Tarasovich, born into a world of secrecy and hidden power. From a young age, he was groomed to take on the responsibilities and burdens of his family's clandestine endeavors. As he grew, Boris proved himself to be a shrewd and intelligent individual, driven by ambition and a desire to restore his family's lost empire.

In his early years, Boris showed a remarkable aptitude for finance and economics. He delved into the intricate workings of the banking system in the Bell Lands, unearthing hidden opportunities and exploiting them to increase his family's wealth. Boris understood that financial power was a key aspect of regaining influence, and he made it his mission to secure House Tarasovich's position in the economic landscape.

However, as Boris delved deeper into his pursuits, a mysterious sickness began to plague him. It started with bouts of persistent coughing, gradually worsening over time. To his horror, he would occasionally expel black bile, a disturbing sign of the underlying ailment. Concerned, Boris sought the guidance of renowned physicians and experts, but none could provide a definitive diagnosis or cure.

Despite his deteriorating health, Boris refused to yield to his sickness. He continued to manage House Tarasovich's financial affairs with unwavering determination, refusing to show any signs of weakness. However, those close to him could see the toll it was taking on his body and spirit.

Boris's condition became an open secret within the confines of House Tarasovich. His family, deeply worried about their scion's well-being, spared no expense in seeking medical assistance. They consulted healers, sought alternative remedies, and even delved into ancient texts and arcane knowledge in the hopes of finding a solution.

As the years passed, Boris's health continued to decline, and the nature of his illness remained a baffling enigma. His once-energetic demeanor was replaced by a weariness that weighed heavily on him. The black bile he coughed up became a somber reminder of the shadowed path he walked.

Despite the personal hardships he faced, Boris remained committed to his family's cause. He orchestrated complex financial maneuvers, navigating the treacherous waters of banking and commerce with a strategic brilliance that belied his failing health. Boris's indomitable spirit and unwavering dedication became a source of inspiration for those around him.

Eventually, the day came when Boris Tarasovich succumbed to his mysterious illness. His passing left a void within House Tarasovich, but also ignited a burning determination within his successors to continue his legacy and fulfill the family's aspirations.

The cause of Boris's sickness remained a haunting mystery, one that troubled House Tarasovich for years to come. Some whispered that it was a consequence of the shadow magic that coursed through their veins, a reminder of the curse they carried. Others speculated that it was a targeted act by enemies who sought to weaken House Tarasovich. But the truth eluded them, buried within the depths of secrecy and shadows.

Boris Tarasovich's name would be remembered as a symbol of resilience and unwavering dedication to the cause. His legacy served as a constant reminder of the sacrifices necessary to regain power and the toll that the pursuit of hidden influence could take on even the strongest of individuals.

# House Staff

- Blessed Geoffrey Fortier was a pellist priest, chosen for his deep understanding of the heretical teachings and his unyielding devotion to the Pellist Heresy. As a spiritual guide and confidant to the members of House Tarasovich, Blessed Geoffrey provided counsel, performed sacred rites, and upheld the principles of the Bellringers. His presence brought solace and reassurance to the family, ensuring that their actions remained aligned with the heretical cause they championed.
- Clapper Bell Jean-Paul and Clapper Rodegro Alvarez were pellist warrior monks, trained in the martial arts and committed to defending the interests of House Tarasovich. Their formidable combat skills were honed through rigorous training and disciplined practice. These warrior monks, clad in the distinctive garb of the Pellist order, became the enforcers of House Tarasovich's will, protecting its secrets and ensuring the safety of its members.
- Bell Jean-Paul possessed a calm and composed demeanor, always striving for perfection in his techniques. His disciplined nature and unwavering loyalty made him a valuable asset in both physical confrontations and tactical planning. Whether it was guarding the family's hidden sanctuaries or executing intricate security protocols, Bell Jean-Paul's presence instilled a sense of confidence and safety among the Tarasovich household.
- Rodegro Alvarez, on the other hand, was known for his fierce determination and relentless pursuit of justice. His fiery spirit, combined with his physical prowess, made him a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. Rodegro's loyalty to House Tarasovich and his commitment to the Pellist cause fueled his unwavering dedication, ensuring that no threat went unanswered and no enemy went unpunished.

# House Secrets

1. The Hidden Treasury: House Tarasovich possesses a well-guarded secret treasury that holds immense wealth accumulated over generations. Deep within the underground catacombs of their summer estate lies a labyrinthine vault, filled with gold, precious gems, and ancient artifacts of immeasurable value. This treasury not only serves as a symbol of House Tarasovich's hidden power but also provides them with the financial means to fund their covert operations and manipulate the political landscape.
2. The Cursed Bloodline: The members of House Tarasovich carry a hereditary curse stemming from their ancestral ties to House Sedi. Known as the "Shadow's Mark," this curse manifests as a corruption of their bloodline, causing a gradual descent into madness and darkness. Those affected by the curse often experience unnerving visions, uncontrollable bursts of shadow magic, or even physical deformities. House Tarasovich guards this secret fiercely, employing rituals, potions, and powerful artifacts to suppress the curse's effects and maintain their semblance of normalcy.
3. The Veiled Oracle: House Tarasovich guards a closely held secret known as the "Veiled Oracle." The Veiled Oracle is an ancient chamber hidden deep within their ancestral estate, accessible only to the chosen few. Within this chamber dwells a mysterious entity—an otherworldly being with the ability to glimpse into the future and provide cryptic, prophetic insights.

- The Veiled Oracle has been consulted by the heads of House Tarasovich for generations, guiding their decisions and strategies in the shadows. The entity's predictions, though often enigmatic, have proven remarkably accurate, enabling House Tarasovich to navigate treacherous political landscapes, foreseeing potential betrayals, and securing advantageous alliances.
- To protect the secrecy of the Veiled Oracle, House Tarasovich has implemented elaborate rituals and powerful wards, ensuring that only the trusted inner circle can access its wisdom. The knowledge gleaned from the Veiled Oracle has become a cornerstone of House Tarasovich's clandestine operations, allowing them to anticipate their enemies' moves and manipulate events to their advantage.
- However, the Veiled Oracle's revelations are not without consequences. Many who have spent extended periods in its presence have been affected by its eldritch energies, experiencing visions and haunting dreams that blur the line between reality and the ethereal realm, and guarantee an advancement of the Shadow's mark.
