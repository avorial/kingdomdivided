House Arstarcsalia - The Silent Navigators

House Arstarcsalia, situated along the coasts and rivers of Montimer's domain, They use small fishing boats and the like to maintain their experince on the sea. Years ago, they where saliors who had the same reputation as the landed knights, but with the loss of the Montimer fleets, they have lost power and influnce. They have been working hard to influnce the courts to reinvest in the fleets and have sent their second sons to be trained in the navel powers of the heartstone.
