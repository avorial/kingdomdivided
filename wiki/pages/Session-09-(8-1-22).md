# Events

The adventure started as the cadre was prepping to leave Esterash to House Derth's domain, before they left they stopped by the blacksmith that they had put a commission on to get armor for Carrick Silverridge, a special breastplate called Cirein-cròin was bought.

From there they came across a group of merchants who had been killed by bandits the bodies were stripped and hidden. The party spent a night in the woods before moving ahead and seeing a group of well armed merchants. One of the least armed asked for help as the party was attacked. The party beat them easily but there was a disagreement with if the least armed was with the bandits or not. They erred on the side that he would not do them any harm and moved on to Bellmore, with a wagon full of Berserker Moss.

They arrive at Bellmore and eventually get inside Vulture's Roost, and meet up with dog Ustus and Beastmaster Andrew Morrey. They meet up with young Starla Derth and the Baroness Emma Derth-McKain they learn that the Saffron  Society has been attacking Bellmoor constantly. Even right now the Snow Kissed unit with Sir Taegan Derth and the Watchdogs under Baron Fredrick Derth.

They also learn that the Saffron Society has been putting special marks hidden on weapons to give them a perk.

Also that a small symbol of the Saffron Society are windchimes in poor homes.

When Taegan Derth returns his brother Cynog Derth start ribbing one another giving insults but making a point to stay far away from anything that would be damaging. He leaves and Cynog Derth secludes himself with the announcement of his father's Baron Fredrick Derth coming. He is with a group of mystics. The game stops and will start with the questions they have of Baron Fredrick Derth
