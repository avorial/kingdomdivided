# History

House Silveroak: A History of Legacy and Conflict

House Silveroak, though not officially recognized as a formal banner under the domain of Whitestar, holds a distinguished position as prominent nobles within the region. They have a long-standing history and deep roots in the land, tracing back to the tribes of the Great Maw. Their namesake city, Silveroak, stands as a testament to their enduring presence and the prosperity they have brought to the region.

The heart of House Silveroak's power lies in their unique and coveted resource—a special lumber that possesses remarkable qualities. This lumber, known as "Silveroak timber," is highly sought after for crafting arrow shafts. When arrows are fashioned from this wood, they acquire an almost magical quality, increasing their accuracy, speed, and impact. This advantage on the battlefield has earned House Silveroak a reputation as formidable archers, and their arrows are highly prized by warriors and hunters alike.

Within the domain of Whitestar, House Silveroak maintains their ancestral villa, known as "Orange Villa," situated along the meandering river that winds through the land. This riverside estate serves as their secondary residence away from the city of Silveroak, reflecting their connection to the waterways and trade routes that have contributed to their wealth and influence over the generations.

Fiona Silveroak, a young and promising member of the house, stands as the heir to House Silveroak's legacy. Fiona's parents, Sir Harrick and Lady Elara Silveroak, have been instrumental in nurturing her growth and preparing her for the responsibilities that await her. Sir Harrick, a respected leader and Peacekeeper, has instilled in Fiona a deep sense of pride in their house's heritage and a commitment to upholding their noble traditions. Lady Elara, known for her grace and wisdom, has nurtured Fiona's diplomatic skills, preparing her to navigate the intricate political landscape of the region.

Despite their esteemed position, House Silveroak faces ongoing conflicts with the ruling House Whitestar. The tensions between the two houses stem from differing ideologies and disagreements over governance. House Silveroak advocates for greater independence for the cities, seeking to empower the common people and revive the old traditions of blood feuds and clan loyalties. In contrast, House Whitestar maintains a policy of enriching trade and attempting to suppress the violent nature of its citizens. These conflicting ideologies have led to strained relations and occasional clashes between the two houses.

House Silveroak also shares an ancient connection to the Fedor people. Legends speak of House Silveroak's ancestors standing side by side with Angus, the great Fedor chieftain, and together they built the first settlements that laid the foundation for their enduring bond. This connection to the Fedor people has influenced House Silveroak's values and traditions, instilling in them a sense of adventure, resilience, and a deep respect for nature.

Known for his unyielding loyalty and tactical brilliance, he has stood at the forefront of countless battles, defending the honor and interests of House Silveroak.

Erik's expertise ensures that the quality of Silveroak timber remains unmatched, solidifying House Silveroak's reputation as the premier suppliers of exceptional arrow shafts.

# House Secrets

## The Ancient Pact:

House Silveroak has an ancient pact with the Old Gods spirits that inhabit the sacred Silveroak trees. These spirits have bestowed the family with unparalleled archery skills and protected their lands for generations. However, the terms of the pact remain shrouded in mystery, and the consequences of breaking or upholding the pact are unknown. Fiona, as the next in line, must unravel the secrets of this pact and make choices that will shape the future of House Silveroak. As this is also a open hersey to the bellringer, it's knowledge is only known to the closest confidants of House Silveroak.

## The Forbidden Love:

Among the annals of House Silveroak's history lies a tale of forbidden love between a Silveroak scion and a member of House Whitestar. This clandestine affair, concealed due to the longstanding rivalry between the houses, resulted in a child who was kept secret, known only to a handful of trusted individuals. The revelation of this hidden bloodline could potentially ignite further conflicts if exposed, as by right the line of Silveroak, may be the true heirs of Angus and the seat of Whitestar!

## Secret: The Mysterious Hole of Silveroak

Deep within the dense forests of Silveroak lies a secret that only a select few are privy to—the existence of a hidden hole, believed to be connected to the ancient abyss known as the Great Maw. This enigmatic chasm, though periodically sealed and concealed, harbors a sinister secret that sends shivers down the spines of those who know of its existence.

Every few generations, against all efforts to seal it permanently, the hole inexplicably opens, unleashing a torrent of dark and malevolent creatures upon the land. These supernatural entities emerge from the depths of the abyss, wreaking havoc and terrorizing the surrounding area. The attacks are swift, merciless, and seemingly unstoppable, leaving the inhabitants of Silveroak in a state of perpetual fear and vulnerability.

Yet, despite the devastation caused by these relentless raids, the true nature and origin of the hole's dark inhabitants remain shrouded in secrecy. Those in positions of power within House Silveroak and certain influential figures in the region are aware of the connection between the hole and the Great Maw. However, they orchestrate a calculated cover-up to prevent panic and maintain a semblance of control over the situation.

The reasons behind the periodic opening of the hole and the release of these malevolent creatures remain a mystery. Speculations range from ancient curses to an underlying force seeking to undermine House Silveroak's influence and authority. Unraveling the truth behind this secret becomes a perilous journey, one that requires tenacity, bravery, and a willingness to confront the lurking darkness that threatens the land.

Only a small circle of trusted individuals within House Silveroak and a handful of outsiders possess fragments of knowledge regarding this clandestine affair. Exposing the truth and putting an end to the horrors unleashed by the hole becomes a daunting task, one that could potentially alter the fate of House Silveroak and the domain of Whitestar forever.
