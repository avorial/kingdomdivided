=History=

A volcanic set of islands off the coast of the Bell Lands. The lands are tropical because of warm ocean winds. The racial group on there are primarily Saltbloods or more mysterious races. They are a Theocratic State, run by their leader of the Prince in Yellow. Their Faith is called Xiūshì.

Their key deity is known as Deipa, but they are also polytheistic, feeling that Deipa is merely the leader of their gods.

The capital of the Saffron Isle is called Iskar, and have multiple ports. The island has many diseases akin to malira, this has protected them from conquest for since the Enisen Kings.
