=History=

Deerholt in history was a hunting town for the nobles of the east, Founded by House Henge. Because of that desire for quaintness it was never overdeveloped. Gradually it became the gift to be given to the second families of the east. Lastly House Rhienhart & House MacBavo. In 175 R.E. a group of bandits attacked the town routing all the nobles. It was then held in bandit control, or Safforn till 189.
