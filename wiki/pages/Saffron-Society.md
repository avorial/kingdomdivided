# History

The Saffron Society, started out as a group of mystic healers from the Saffron Isles. They did not charge for their medical care feeling that it was part of their religious crusade to help those who had no other aid. On the face of it, few could complain of helping the lower classes that could not afford medical attention it grew in to a mystical cult saying that the best aid that could be given to the poor would be to eliminate the nobility.

They preach the "Bell Ringer" is just a slave master, and must be overthrown with all of his agents on earth. For years they quietly infiltrated poor groups building a secret militia, teaching farmers and peddlers combat techniques. Their spiritual leader is Samson Kreeg a Bellringer missionary who was converted to the Saffron faith. He has learned their heresy magics (the following of the god Kilat), and uses them to keep ahead of the Imperial peacekeepers.  Many of the nobles houses fear Saffron interference in their serfs, as their sickle symbol has been seen all through the empire.

Military they are broken in to 3 main groups based on the colors the flower grows.

The Greens - Peasant Farmers that are just willing to fight and die for a better life.

The Purples - Trained Mercenaries or Professional Hunters that are the elites of the combat units

The Reds - Purples who have reached a point where they truly of the faith and are either embued with minor magics or are leaders of other units.

The Golds - Priests or Magi that run units or groups.
