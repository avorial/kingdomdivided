# History

The Thrani, a venerable race existing on the sphere alongside the Avestan, trace their origins to a time shrouded in antiquity. Their founder, known as the Patient One, played a pivotal role in the last iteration of Oh. Anticipating the impending devouring of worlds, the Patient One, armed with both magical prowess and profound knowledge, made a conscious choice to distance himself from the burgeoning Oh. The awareness that he, too, would eventually fall victim to consumption fueled his actions.

In a display of magical mastery, the Patient One cast a spell upon himself, a protective enchantment that not only ensured his survival amidst the cosmic digestion but also left an indelible mark on his form – golden skin, a testament to the arcane forces at play. As the eons unfolded during the process of cosmic digestion, where countless entities lost their essence and identity, the Patient One exhibited a remarkable patience, steadfast in his commitment to outlast the inevitable fate that awaited him.

Eventually, after an immeasurable span of time, the Patient One's journey brought him to the blood shores of a new world. Utilizing his arcane abilities, he undertook the task of resurrecting his old allies, companions who had succumbed to the devouring forces but were still tethered to him by a shared history and kinship. The Patient One, undeterred by the challenges of this new realm, embarked on the reclamation of his people.

The Thani, brought back to life by the Patient One's unwavering determination, flourished alongside the Avestan in this uncharted territory. In a symbiotic relationship with the Avestan, the Thrani emerged as the thinkers and wielders of ancient magics, complementing the Avestan who took on the role of shaping the nascent world. Together, they formed a harmonious alliance, contributing their unique strengths to the coalescence of a great nation in this unfamiliar realm.

The Patient One, a guiding force among the Thani, continued to lead with patience and wisdom. His enduring commitment to his people and their shared destiny created a sense of unity and purpose. The Thani, now under the leadership of the Patient One, thrived as custodians of ancient knowledge and keepers of mystical traditions that predated the creation of their new world.

As the Thani and the Avestan collaborated, a profound synergy emerged, marking the birth of a civilization shaped by the fusion of magical prowess and creative shaping. The golden-skinned Thani, with their deep connection to ancient magics, became the custodians of the ethereal, weaving spells that resonated with the echoes of epochs long past.

However, the inevitable arrived, and the Patient One, having fulfilled his great task of ushering the old world into the new, faced his final death. The Thani, numbering 70,000, gathered to witness the passing of their venerable leader. In his last moments, the Patient One imparted his wisdom and legacy to his people, ensuring that the flame of their shared history continued to burn brightly.

The Thani, now without the direct guidance of their founder, embraced their newfound autonomy. Drawing inspiration from the Patient One's teachings, they continued to cultivate their role as custodians of ancient magics and thinkers of the highest order. The golden-skinned race forged ahead, navigating the challenges and opportunities of their world with a sense of purpose instilled by their venerable leader.

In the absence of the Patient One, the Thani and the Avestan forged deeper bonds, each contributing to the cultural tapestry of their shared civilization. The Thani's golden skin became a symbol of their resilience, a testament to their ability to endure and adapt in the face of cosmic upheaval.
