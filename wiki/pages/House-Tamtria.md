House Tamtria - Keepers of the Black Glass

Originating from a group of survivors shipwrecked in the tumultuous times preceding the crusades, House Tamtria's history is steeped in resilience and a dark secret. Initially fleeing Esten King troops, they sought refuge in the old ruins where they discovered an enigmatic material known as Black Glass. As they extracted this unique substance, they observed its transformative effects on those who wielded it. The more it was utilized, the more it influenced the individuals, granting immense strength but at the cost of unleashing a profound rage within them.

Facing the challenge of maintaining stability in the face of the material's unpredictable impact, House Tamtria devised a system where individuals took turns wielding the Black Glass to mitigate its adverse effects. This delicate balance allowed the society to endure, albeit at the cost of individual sanity. With time, they harnessed the stones in controlled amounts, realizing that moderation could harness the strength without succumbing entirely to the accompanying fury.

During the liberation by the armies of Ethen Sedi, the use of Black Glass was forbidden, a decree intended to safeguard the mental well-being of House Tamtria's inhabitants. However, the allure of the stones proved too powerful to resist, and the forbidden practice persisted in secret. The duality of Black Glass, offering both incredible power and a perilous descent into madness, became an integral part of Tamtria's identity. Their struggle to balance this dark material mirrors their broader quest for equilibrium in the face of external threats and internal challenges. As Keepers of the Black Glass, House Tamtria walks a precarious line between strength and chaos, with the mysterious substance shaping both their history and their uncertain future.
