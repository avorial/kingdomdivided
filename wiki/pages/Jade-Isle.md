=History=

The Jade Isle is named for the vertant jungles that cover almost every inch of land. 2 Mountains are the only landmark that breaks the line of trees. The legend of this lands creation is that the island itself was a killed Star Gods bested by during the treaty of the Worm Gods and the Old. It crashed in to the blood sea and became the paradise it is today. While fables are fables, the reality is less pleasant. There is not enough farmland for the population so most of the poor are fed on a bark Qook. It's a mostly tasteless with a slight bitter flavor. One might wonder though with all the vertant fruits and animals why the poor eat this way.

The reason is trade, a majority of the foodstuff beyond Qook is very flavorful fruits that are perfect for long sea voyages. It's wild animals are the amusement of nobles across the globe. For this, only nobles in the Jade Isle gets to commonly partake of it's bounty. House Ogham has tried over the generations to move their trade more to other resources but either by fate or the whims of the gods, no plan seems to stick. King Roki Ogham's plan is to try to make the Jade Isle in to shipyards, but it will time to see if he can break the cycle that has beset this isle.
