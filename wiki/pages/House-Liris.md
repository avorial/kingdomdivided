### **House Liris**

![house_liris](/wiki/media/house_liris.png)

**Symbol:** A black banner adorned with the head of Oh, the bringer of mysteries, shining with a black light  
**Words:** "In Shadows, We See"

House Liris is a house shrouded in enigma and reverence for the unknown. They believe that Ethan Sedi, the conqueror of the Esten Kings, is a dark god of shadows, an entity of profound mystery and power, imbued by the old gods to be a gateway between the stars and the land. This belief has shaped their identity and practices, leading them to adopt and transform the Sedi banner into a symbol of their own devotion and interpretation.

#### **History and Beliefs**

The fall of the Esten Kings marked a significant turning point for House Liris. Unlike other houses that sought to preserve their old ways or flee to distant lands, House Liris embraced a new spiritual path. They saw in Ethan Sedi not just a conqueror, but a deity of shadows and secrets. This revelation led them to reinterpret their place in the world, aligning their purpose with the exploration and mastery of the unknown.

Their adoption of the Sedi banner, altered to feature the head of Oh—a symbol of the unknowable and the mysterious—signified their transformation. The stars that once adorned the Sedi banner were replaced, representing a departure from the celestial and a deeper dive into the arcane and shadowy aspects of existence.

#### **Culture and Characteristics**

House Liris is characterized by their pursuit of knowledge, particularly that which is hidden or obscured. Their members are often scholars, seers, and mystics, deeply engaged in uncovering the secrets of the world and beyond. This house places a high value on wisdom and cunning, often engaging in activities that require subtlety and intelligence. Those that lack the wealth for such things try to live the simple life of following the destiny given to them, thinking that the message of Ethen Sedi will guide them.

**Traditions:**

- **The Night of Veils:** An annual ritual where the members of House Liris gather to share the mysteries they've uncovered over the past year. This event includes cryptic storytelling, shadow plays, and the unveiling of new discoveries and theories about the nature of shadows and the dark god Ethan Sedi.
- **The Rite of Oh's Gaze:** A ceremonial practice in which young initiates must meditate in total darkness, seeking visions and insights from the head of Oh. This rite is a test of their ability to perceive and interpret the unseen, a crucial skill for members of House Liris.

#### **Current Standing**

House Liris occupies a unique niche among the surviving houses of the fallen Esten Empire. Their expertise in the arcane and their reputation as keepers of forbidden knowledge have earned them both respect and suspicion. They maintain a network of informants and allies, often trading secrets and intelligence to maintain their influence.

Their symbol, the head of Oh shining with black light, serves as a reminder of their devotion to the mysteries that lie in the darkness. It is a beacon for those who seek to delve into the unknown, offering guidance and protection through the enigmatic wisdom of House Liris.

In conclusion, House Liris stands as a beacon of the unseen and the mysterious. Their reverence for the dark god Ethan Sedi and their pursuit of hidden knowledge set them apart, making them both revered and feared. In shadows, they find clarity, and through their understanding of the unknown, they carve a path of power and enlightenment.
