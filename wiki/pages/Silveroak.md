# History:

## Founding and Early Days:

Silveroak, a city with a rich and storied history, was founded centuries ago by the ancestors of House Silveroak. The city's roots trace back to the tribes of the Great Maw, who recognized the unique qualities of the Silveroak timber found in the surrounding forests. This exceptional lumber, prized for crafting arrow shafts with near-magical properties, provided the foundation for the city's prosperity and the emergence of House Silveroak as prominent nobles in the region.

## Key Events and Rise to Prominence:

As the years passed, Silveroak flourished under the careful stewardship of House Silveroak. The city's reputation as a center for skilled archers grew, attracting warriors, hunters, and traders from near and far. The Silveroak archers gained a formidable reputation on the battlefield, thanks to their deadly arrows crafted from the renowned Silveroak timber. During this period, House Silveroak solidified their connections with the Sedi Empire, with the rise of the Rivers Empire they delivered a throne made from their lumber, this is seen as the moonlight throne and is used by the Rivers Emperor any time he must travel outside of Oldwyn.

Many people in Silveroak have a sigil on their home noting what ancestor they are related to from Angus' tribesmen. Conflicts and Rivalries: Throughout its history, Silveroak has faced conflicts and rivalries, particularly with the ruling House Whitestar. Tensions arose due to differing ideologies, with House Silveroak advocating for greater independence for the cities and the revival of old traditions, while House Whitestar pursued enriching trade and suppressing violence. This has made the town seen as the bastard child of Whitestar's lands. Never rich enough to help the Dutchy never poor enough to fully ignore.

## Hole of Silveroak:

Deep within the dense forests surrounding Silveroak lies a mysterious chasm connected to the ancient abyss known as the Great Maw. This hole, though periodically sealed, occasionally opens, unleashing malevolent creatures that terrorize the region. House Silveroak and influential figures within the realm work to keep the existence of this dark secret from the public, as they search for answers behind its origins and purpose.

## Present Times:

Today, Silveroak remains a bustling city, known for its skilled archers, rich trade, and vibrant culture. Under the leadership of Sir Harrick Silveroak and with Lady Fiona Silveroak as the promising heir, the house continues to uphold its legacy and navigate the complex political landscape of the region.
