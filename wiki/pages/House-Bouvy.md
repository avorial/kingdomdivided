House Bouvy - The Falcon's Eyes

House Bouvy has a unique connection with the avian companions that grace the skies. Falconry is not just a pastime for them; it's a tradition passed down through generations. These skilled falconers use their feathered allies for scouting, communication, and even in ceremonies. The eyes of the falcon are the watchful guardians of House Bouvy, providing unparalleled insights into the ever-shifting dynamics of Montimer's surroundings. Their connection to these birds was built up from centuries of breading, making these falcons able to be tamed and trained in weeks while a falcon from elsewhere in the coutnry could take years.
