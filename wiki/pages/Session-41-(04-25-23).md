# Events

## Brothers from across the sea

Tallion Ramirez lands off the north river with 4 units of troops, after a days march he is captured by Douglas Sherman's troops and taken to Ashbourne. A Messager is sent to each of the armies, to have their representive come and decide on what they are going to do about the Ramirez troops, as they are a overt violation of the compact of not having troops cross over domains. The messenger gets to the Cadre, and they enter the town, seeing for the first time the Crimson Guard File:Crimson Guard.jpg|thumb|Crimson Guard, a unit of tested militants.

Entering the tower they see, Endulaudaus File:Endulaudaus.jpg|thumb, and Soverighn Ides Lev Tarasovich after some debate, Soverighn puts forth that they just take it to a vote. (knowing that if this takes too long to decide that Titus or his proxy may return.) The vote allows the troops to go in to House Silverridge units. The push for Tallion Ramirez's troops to be paid as mercenaries is off putting to many as they pass off that he has to talk to Connor about it.

## Connor's Deal

After the group is taken before Connor (Who says he knows Tallion), he offers first access to the north river for trade, when the war is finished. And 10 gold for every unit that the pirates destroy more then 51% of.
