Arrival

Peter arrives at the nondescript house and is confronted by Jarreth, a mercenary that his been in charge of protecting Peter’s mother for many years.  After confronting Peter and being satisfied that Peter is as he claims to be, Jarrett lets Peter in. Peter meets Thalassa Ember, Rosalind’s nurse and Freya.  Being suspicious of Freya as she is a newcomer Peter asks her a number of questions and tests her story as best he is able.

Peter sits with his mother through the night. He hears his mother mumbling, she gets up and screams “Beware the stars, for they shall weep blood and rain fire upon the land. The moon shall turn as black as coal, and the sun shall hide its face from the world. In the darkest hour, a shadow shall rise, born of chaos and madness, with eyes like twin abysses that devour hope.” As morning comes he quietly describes beautiful things he’s seen and she reveals knows where the Griffens roost in Gesdessas and then reveals On Sanctuary lies the sleeping Phoenix, he will be the light for the hero he follows.

Rosalind also tells Peter that O’rih knows all of Malcom’s secrets.  He also learns that Endelycious was previously called Ephrayim. As Ephrayim he was charming and intelligent and would keep his mother company when Edward was not around.

Rosalind has a second rant which sounds like scripture or prophecy-

“A forgotten city, lost to time, shall emerge from the depths, and its gates shall open to release the terrors of old. The serpent's eye shall pierce the heavens, and the world shall be cloaked in an eternal night.”

Peter speaks with Jarreth and learns the man’s squire is ready to learn horsecraft so he can enter tournaments and become a proper knight. Peter offers to take the boy with him.
