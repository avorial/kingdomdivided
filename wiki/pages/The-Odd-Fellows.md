# History

The Odd Fellows started in the first few years after the rise of the Rivers Empire, starting originally with refuges from the old retainers of the Sedi court they took in many refuges from the those that pushed back learning. In even a decade the group had so many different voices that unified origin was lost.

The tradition started that there was Petitioners and Members, Those who were coming for aid, that those that would give it. Everyone who was not a member would wear a mask showing that everyone was allowed to keep their backgrounds as hidden as they would like.
