# Events

## Wild Oats

When the Cadre returns from the High Tea, the male members decide they will take Carrick Silverridge to a local brothel. Realizing their finances are not what they would hope them to be, they ask Duke Conner Silverridge to aid them, he supports them and tells them that Doamna Bree Liban will take them to the brothel as she has a friend who runs it.

When they arrive to the riverboat, she introduces them to Lady Anne the owner. She gets them set up, and as everyone participates, Casimir Ramirez stays longer and makes a contact with a covenguard girl named Clarise.

## Enisen Justice

Lyla Silverridge sneaks off to go and talk to Dutchess Mercy Talmert to try to find out if she set up the person to take the fall for her in killing General Paz Rodrego. Mercy says she did not pay him or was he guilty of something what she did pay is the people who could have freed him, she took away that chance from him. Lyla starting to understand the brutality of the world they are in accepts Mercy's statement and returns moments before the boys come home.

## Damsel In Distress

The next morning before Casimir Ramirez returns they hear a fight in the hallway, when they get out they see a man Dennis Durand being pulled away by Peacekeeper Noel Lombardo and castle guards. Lyla Silverridge is able to get the story out of Noel, that Dennis tried to kill his cousin, for his father who there is a disagreement on who should hold House Durand's domains.

Baroness Penelope Durand who is wounded is brought in by House Silverridge and they tend her wounds. They remark how they are flush with family and how regretful, Penelope Durand does not have any to come to her aid.

## Plans upon Plans

With Casimir Ramirez's return, They all meet with Duke Conner Silverridge and he says the next problem at hand is they need to make sure to have one of the 3 General titles offered for this crusade.

Casimir Ramirez he plans to put in charge of the House Silverridge troops.

Caden Mattix &amp; Lyla Silverridge He is going to want to run individual units to make attacks on the Saffron's

He notes that he is going to be meeting with Endulaudaus to before the meeting and try to get him on his side, the players decide to go with him.

## Backroom Deals

They go to the halls of the tested and are met by Learned Lady Agnes Martain who entertains them for a moment while Endulaudaus finishes his next meeting, While they do Lyla Silverridge remarks that their carpet has sigils on it that remind her of the Enisen Kings lay lines, she does not hide it but does not elaborate.

They are brought in to meet with Endulaudaus &amp; Walsink a member of the privy council.

Endulaudaus notes that while Conner Silverridge has the support of

*Silverridge

*Almirn

*Whitestar

They need one more to be a general, And as Montmier will not bow before them, who will they convince? Connor feels that the Talmert will support him as it's the only house that has not gone to war with the Enisen Kings.

As the conversation settles, Conner Silverridge gives Endulaudaus a star stone from his travels to bribe him for his support.

As they leave they see the next group of guards are House Tarasovich in House Covinggard uniforms.

The cadre return and talk about marriage choices and how they will control the east.
