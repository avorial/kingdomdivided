# History

House Ramirez, the federation of pirate captains, continues to thrive and exert its influence on the vast seas. While piracy remains a core aspect of their operations, they have also adapted to the changing times and diversified their activities to include legitimate trade, smuggling, and privateering. House Ramirez has established a reputation for their audacious exploits, unparalleled naval prowess, and a network of secret bases and strongholds scattered throughout the maritime territories.

Under the leadership of Captain Sofia Ramirez, the current first among equals of House Ramirez, the federation has expanded its reach and solidified its position as a major player in the seafaring world. Sofia's shrewd business acumen has allowed House Ramirez to maintain a delicate balance between their illicit activities and strategic partnerships with various factions, both within and outside the realm of piracy. Through carefully orchestrated maneuvers and discreet alliances, House Ramirez has managed to elude the full force of retribution from powerful nations while amassing wealth and resources.

Captain Ricardo Ramirez, Sofia's brother, has played a crucial role in House Ramirez's ongoing success. His expertise in exploration and cartography has enabled the federation to discover new trade routes, hidden islands, and uncharted territories. These discoveries have not only provided House Ramirez with valuable resources and strategic advantages but have also allowed them to establish secret bases and trading posts in secluded locations, evading the attention of authorities and rival pirate groups.

House Ramirez operates on a code of loyalty and unity, with its pirate captains bound by a deep sense of camaraderie and shared purpose. The federation serves as a hub for information exchange, resource pooling, and mutual support among its members. This unity grants House Ramirez a formidable edge, as their combined forces can swiftly assemble to defend their interests or launch coordinated attacks against common enemies.

While House Ramirez maintains a degree of autonomy and independence, they remain closely associated with House Silverridge, to whom they owe their historical allegiance. The House Ramirez captains often lend their naval expertise and support to House Silverridge's campaigns, providing crucial maritime assistance and acting as a powerful deterrent against rival forces.

In the present era, House Ramirez stands as a force to be reckoned with on the open seas, their legacy of piracy and maritime prowess continuing to shape their reputation. Through their intricate network of alliances, hidden bases, and strategic maneuvers, they remain a constant thorn in the side of authorities and a symbol of resilience and audacity in the face of adversity. House Ramirez's influence reaches far and wide, their golden sails and fierce reputation striking fear into the hearts of those who dare to cross their path.

## The Silverridge Bastards

The minor branch of House Ramirez, known as the Silverridge Bastards, plays a unique role as diplomats and intermediaries between House Silverridge and House Ramirez. The influence of the Duchy of Silverridge can not be ignored, strong alliances and relationships have formed between members of both houses, the Silverridge Bastards embody a blend of Silverridge nobility and Ramirez blood. A saying goes: A Ramirez who can not swim does not know his father.

# Secrets

- The Shadow Syndicate: Deep within the secretive operations of House Ramirez, there exists a clandestine organization known as the Shadow Syndicate. Composed of influential pirate captains and underworld figures from across the seas, the Shadow Syndicate operates as a covert network that facilitates smuggling, information brokering, and high-profile heists. Led by the enigmatic figure known as "The Whisper," this shadowy group has its fingers in many pies, orchestrating covert operations that often go unnoticed by the authorities. - "The Whisper" (Real Name: Valeria Shadowspire) - A master manipulator and mastermind behind the Shadow Syndicate. Valeria possesses an uncanny ability to gather information and pull strings from the shadows. Her reputation for staying several steps ahead of her adversaries and orchestrating intricate schemes has made her both feared and respected within House Ramirez.
- The Cursed Relic: House Ramirez guards a closely held secret tied to a mysterious artifact, known as the "Heart of the Sea." This cursed relic is said to grant immense power to its possessor but at a great cost. Legends claim that the heart can manipulate the tides and control the winds, allowing the one who possesses it to dominate the seas. However, those who seek its power are plagued by misfortune and tragedy. House Ramirez has carefully guarded the relic, using it sparingly and only entrusting its safekeeping to their most trusted members. - Captain Isabella Stormrider - A fiercely independent and daring pirate captain known for her relentless pursuit of treasure and glory. Isabella has been tasked by House Ramirez with safeguarding the "Heart of Davos" and ensuring its secrets remain hidden from those who would seek to exploit its power. Her loyalty to the house and her unwavering determination make her a formidable ally and a feared adversary on the high seas.
- The Smuggler's Cove: House Ramirez operates a hidden haven known as the Smuggler's Cove, a secluded island in the far north tucked away from prying eyes and authorities. The cove serves as a bustling hub for illicit trade, covert meetings, and secret dealings. Here, pirate captains from various factions come together to exchange information, acquire rare contraband, and forge alliances in a neutral ground that ensures their safety and anonymity. - Madam Serafina "Silverscale" Drakefang - A charismatic and influential figure within House Ramirez, Madam Serafina runs the notorious Smuggler's Cove. Known for her sharp wit, business acumen, and extensive network of contacts, she navigates the treacherous waters of piracy and smuggling with finesse. Madam Serafina's connections and wealth of knowledge make her an indispensable asset to House Ramirez, as well as a formidable negotiator and mediator within the pirate community.
