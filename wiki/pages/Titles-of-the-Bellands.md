# Nobility

- Imperial Court - Emperor of the Bells (Status 10) - Prince of the Bells (Heir) (Status 9) - Archduke/Archduchess (Other Heir's in the Imperial Family) (Status 8) - King of the Bells (Prime Minister) (Status 9) - Marquis of the Bells (The Privy Council, Currently All Tested) (Status 8)
- Domain Titles - Duke/Duchess (Status 7) - Earl (an Heir of multiple Dukeships or a Honorary title given to a sibling of a Duke) (Status 6) - Count/Countess (the Heir if they have a child, Sometimes also bestowed to an Heir of a Duke that has done a great task) (Status 5) - Baron/Baroness (the Heir if they don't have a child, Sometimes given to other children in the line of succession) (Status 5) - Count/Countess (Status 5) - Baron (the Heir) (Status 3) - Viscount/Viscountess (an Heir of multiple Baronies or a Honorary title given to a sibling of a Duke) (Status 4) - - Sir/Lady (is automatically conferred to any child of a Baron) (Status 2) - Baronet (A title given by a Duke to a family that would be given a Barony if they could) (Status 3) - Domn/Doamna (Mister/Mistress Consort) (Status 2) - * Commoners are only those who have Status 1 - * Adding "Blood" Before the title means that you would have been the one to receive it but either by finishing with the tested or giving up your title. For example "Nathan Epers Blood Baron of the Waysmith."

# Religion

The status of a religious leader can not be used at all on a person not of that faith. They are considered to be one status to a person of an opposing faith.

## Church of the Bells

- Sounder of the Bell (Patriarch of the Bell Lands) (Status 6) - Cardinal of the Crown (Head of the Crown Priest Order) (Status 5) - Grand Master of Clapper (Head of the Militant Clapper Order) (Status 4) - Vicar General of the Faithful Mouth (Head of the Monastic order of the Mouth) (Status 4)
- Starlord (Head of the Church in any Dutchy) (Status 3) - Blessed (Crown Priests) (Status 2) - Chaplin (Clapper Knights) (Status 2) - Brother (Mouth Monks) (Status 2)

- The Master of the Summer (Head of the Stone Gods)
- The Master of the Fall (Head of the Water Gods)
- The Master of the Winter (Head of the Shadow Gods) - Speakers (Common Priest of the Old Gods)

# Peacekeeper Judiciary

- Justicar of the Bells (Head of the Peacekeepers / Elected) (Status 6) - Consul of the Bells (Formally the Head of the Peacekeeper Training School) (Status 5)
- Shophet (The head of a cadre of peacekeepers / Elected) (Status 4) - Peacekeeper (Any peacekeeper who has his sword) (Status 4)

# Imperial Army

- Warlord of the Bells (Head of the Imperial Army) (Status 8) - General of the Bells (those in charge of at least 200 men away from the main army) (Status 5) - Captain of the Bells (Those in charge of at least 1 unit) (Status 2) - Chosen (Anyone who has served or is currently serving in the Imperial army)

# Tested

- Ematha (Head of the Tested in a dutchy) (Status 5)  - Learned (Anyone Who has gotten 5 rings and is a full member of the Tested) (Status 3) - Entrant (A person who has gained at least 1 ring, this is normally only used by commoners) (Status 2)
