# History:

In the vast tapestry of ancient legends, the Quintill people emerged as a proud and enigmatic race, their origins veiled in myth and speculation. According to Quintill lore, their ancestors were blessed by the Star Gods, celestial beings who bestowed upon them their fair hair and pale skin. Legends speak of a pivotal moment when a Quintill was chosen to speak directly to the returning Star Gods, solidifying their connection and influence.

Renowned for their innate leadership qualities, the Quintill became synonymous with visionaries and guiding lights. Whether navigating the intricacies of power in grand halls or plotting behind closed doors, their ability to inspire and their natural inclination for diplomacy positioned them as natural rulers. Through times of turmoil and uncertainty, they steered their people with wisdom and grace, earning the reputation of being a steadying force in the midst of chaos.

# Stereotypes:

The Quintill are widely regarded as benevolent and wise leaders, admired for their fair judgment and impartiality. Their diplomatic prowess enables them to resolve conflicts through negotiation, seeking peaceful resolutions rather than resorting to force. However, the Quintill possess an inherent need for intellectual stimulation and problem-solving. In the absence of challenges, they may succumb to vices or even create problems for themselves to overcome, driven by their insatiable thirst for purpose.

# Biological Traits:

The Quintill possess striking physical traits that set them apart from other races. Their fair hair, imbued with an almost ethereal radiance, ranges from soft golden hues to luminous platinum shades. Complementing their hair is their pale complexion, which seems to emit a gentle, otherworldly glow. Quintill are often tall and slender, exuding an aura of grace and elegance in their movements. While they may not possess great physical strength, their unparalleled agility and dexterity make them formidable in combat. In battle, Quintill warriors favor slender blades, showcasing their finesse and precision.

# Language:

The Quintill language is a melodic and harmonious symphony of words, reflecting their deep affinity for arts and culture. Their speech carries a rhythmic quality, captivating listeners with its soothing resonance. Quintill names are often adorned with poetic elements, celebrating beauty and grace and evoking a sense of awe and admiration.

## Blood of the Quintill:

The "Blood of the Quintill" quality signifies the inherent charisma and leadership qualities that flow through the veins of the Quintill.

Sword Chronicle - Characters possessing this quality gain a +2 bonus to Intimidate and Persuasion tests, representing their innate ability to sway others through charisma and authority. Additionally, when engaging in intrigues or political maneuverings, the Quintill's Status is considered one rank higher, allowing them to influence targets without necessarily entering into full-fledged intrigues.

Burning Wheel - Characters of Quintill lineage gain +1D6 to Intimdate and Persuasion tests, representing their innate ability to sway others through their charisma and authority. Additionally, when engaging in intrigues or political maneuverings, they may either add +1D6 to their Duel of Wits or reduce the Obstacle by -1D6, no lower than 1 Obstacle.

# Inspiration Concept:

The Quintill draw inspiration from the refined courts of Northern France and the opulent era of the Sun King. They embody the ideals of sophistication, elegance, and cultural refinement. The grandeur and allure of these historical periods resonate within Quintill society, shaping their values and customs. They strive to emulate the grace and savoir-faire of the courts, fostering an atmosphere of intellectual pursuits, artistic appreciation, and the pursuit of refined tastes.

Overall, the Quintill are a race characterized by their innate leadership qualities, diplomatic finesse, and refined sensibilities. Their fair appearance and natural talents make them influential figures, admired and respected for their ability to inspire, guide, and lead others towards a more harmonious and enlightened future.
