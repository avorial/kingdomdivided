# Events

After giving the information of the Telung Gods cultists on the hill, the players learn that a Bellringer Priest Ringer Philip Nokum has come to investigate what was happening, fearful of them being called heretics they leave him as soon as possible. While he was being introduced, Carrick Silverridge and Shivon Silverridge go and talk to the House Montmier diplomat, Rhys Bennet, he seems to know already what is happening with the cultists all with answers that  are a little too good. But he does offer to give them passports to Valhurst Castle in Montmier if they wanted to investigate the cults.

They then go to the local bar, and find out there is a old man Doctor Bruce Ulman who is treating a noble lady in the back, Lyla Silverridge is given a note after she does a kind jester for her, with a silver maple leaf thanking her for her discretion. Gregory ap Bentil stays behind and learns that the woman being treated is Lady Autumn Walsh diplomat from House Whitestar.  As the book on cultists talked about House Almirn they then decide to go to the Somchai Frick the House Almirn diplomats house.

When they arrive there they meet his son, and they have some foreign foods, Shivon Silverridge gradually leads the conversation to the cult, where the diplomat says that the The Diary of Ruisín Ó Duinn that they got information from is just propaganda. Even to the point of Lyla noticing he had an idol of Langosta. (he was obviously lying) the conversation then moves to the Saffron Society and how their rise is because of of failures of the Rivers Empire, and he wishes they would improve. He offers to send warships to escort them to Esterash, and seal up their domain of House Wycliff there.
