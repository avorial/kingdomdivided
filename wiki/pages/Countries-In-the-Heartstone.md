There are a few major countries in the heartstone.

1.  Kadal the Undying Lands
    1.  They are the ancient empire that was the first that awoke after O death. They ruled the entire sphere until the Heartbeat and their empire was broken. They have established a large domain, ruled by undying lich kings.
2.  The Belllands
    1.  After being exiled from Figior they did a crusade and took over the Esten Homelands. Making it the Belllands.
3.  Figior
    1.  A religious theocracy of the Star Gods
4.  Mikado
    1.  A empire with a shogun like second state, that follows the mysterious worm gods.
5.  The Ledain League
    1.  A collocation of people who survived the Heartbeat and made a new culture after it.
