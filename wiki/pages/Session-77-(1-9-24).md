# Synopsis

In this series of events, Peter wakes up to mysterious mumbling in his room, emanating from a box containing a mask with lips. After putting on the mask, Peter unintentionally speaks in an unfamiliar language. He then places the mask back in the box, leading to a breakfast debate among the group on what to do with the mysterious mask and who to consult about it.

Deciding to seek guidance, the group visits Esther Kovács, the head of the Tested in Montimier. During the meeting, they discuss the masks' function in the Maw for communication and inquire about Sezed's use of his mask. Casimir expresses a concern about the masks and asks about ways to defeat their wearers. Esther reveals that Endy's former master spent seven years living in the Maw during his youth and survived.

As part of their journey, the group is asked to escort Esther's peacekeeper friend, Julien Baptiste. Casimir purchases a rouncey, and they meet Julien. The journey takes an eventful turn when Peter falls down a cliff, but everyone manages to save him, including his horse. Siobhan gets stung by a scorpion, and the group faces an attack by a bear that leaves Maisy injured with a broken leg.

As the situation escalates, Peter's mask begins speaking, leading the group to a confrontation with Maw creatures watching them. An argument ensues over the mask's significance and purpose. Eventually, they reach Mullrose, where the wounded are treated, and they have a day to rest. During this time, they connect with Julien, and the men are invited to dinner with the Upperton's.

At dinner, Julien and Casimir discuss Julien's history, with Julien subtly trying to shift Casimir's opinion of Conner. The story takes a turn when Julien investigates a criminal accused of being a spy. The criminal claims to be a brigand who stole a cart and provides information about traps set against the Almerin. Casimir argues that a true brigand would have checked the cart's contents.

The situation becomes tense, leading to an execution of the criminal, identified as a pirate trader from the pirate isles. The group then attends dinner with the royals, where they are impressed with Clarissa, and they learn about the upcoming arrival of Tayna Bennet. The events unfold with a mix of intrigue, danger, and interpersonal dynamics, setting the stage for further twists in the story.
