=History=

Langosta, is not the only god of the sea but it one of the most understandable to those who sail the oceans.

==Taboos==

Some or all of these are done by those who are the most pious followers of the Langosta

# Never going a full lunar cycle without seeing a body of water.

# Never Eating Land animals

# Using Sharp weapons

# Throwing offerings in to water.

==Followers==

There is no defined churches of Langosta, most of his followers are those who have learned his secrets from other sailors, or from whispers on windy nights at sea.

===Heresy===

The most noteworthy Hersey is known as the Lost followers of the Lost feel that Langosta is merely an avatar of a god, that is much greater in the darkness of the sea. That this great god birth all of reality out of a dark cave, all land only exists because this god is retreating from us, and thus pulling the water away. The end of the world will come when it is finally so disgusted with humanity that it will return bringing the sea higher then the mountains with it.
