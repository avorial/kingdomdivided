# History:

In the annals of ancient legends, the Paltris people emerge as a resilient and self-reliant race, deeply connected to the Star Gods who guided them through their journey. Legends speak of the Paltris clinging closely to the celestial beings, their bronzed skin a testament to the light they carried within. From the snow-capped peaks of towering mountains to the dense depths of unforgiving jungles, the Paltris venture where others fear to tread, bringing the light and their indomitable spirit to every corner of the world. Their extraordinary adaptability and independent nature have allowed them to establish thriving communities in the most remote and challenging regions, far removed from the bustling centers of the Bell Lands.

The Paltris have honed their skills and forged a unique path through the obstacles presented by their chosen habitats. They have learned to overcome the harshest of conditions, adapting to extreme climates and developing survival techniques to thrive where others struggle. This resilience and self-sufficiency have become defining aspects of their cultural identity, instilling a deep sense of pride within the Paltris people.

# Stereotypes:

The Paltris are widely regarded as a hale and hearty bunch, admired for their ability to flourish in regions that would deter others. They embrace the solitude and ruggedness of their chosen environments, finding strength in the face of adversity. Known for their resilience, they are seen as individuals who possess an unwavering determination and a tenacious spirit not commonly found among the inhabitants of the Bell Lands. When communities of Paltris gather, they become introspective and devout, their connection to the celestial realms leading them to be natural leaders in the realm of faith and spirituality.

# Biological Traits:

The Paltris possess dusky skin, reminiscent of Spanish or North African heritage. This unique complexion serves as a natural protection against the elements they frequently encounter. Their skin, bronzed by their close connection to the Star Gods, reflects their ability to adapt to various environments, providing them with enhanced resilience against the sun's rays, extreme temperatures, and other environmental hazards. This distinctive trait not only contributes to their physical fortitude but also adds to their captivating appearance.

## Blood of the Paltris:

In game mechanics and rules, the Paltris exemplify their hardiness and resilience.

Sword Chronicle - When attacked by environmental hazards, they add their Athletics rank to passive Endurance, showcasing their ability to endure and withstand the challenges presented by their surroundings. Furthermore, in tests of resilience or when foraging and hunting in their natural habitats, Paltris characters can re-roll a number of 1s equal to their Will rank, embodying their ability to adapt and persevere in the face of adversity. The Paltris possess a unique cultural aspect related to their status and engagement with others. Characters with higher Status can never automatically compel Paltris individuals, requiring them to engage in an intrigue to exert influence. This represents the Paltris' independent nature and their preference for personal agency and self-determination. They value direct engagement and open discourse, rather than being swayed by authority or social standing alone.

Burning Wheel - When attacked by environmental hazards, Paltrist's may FoRK in Althletic-related Skills for an additional +1D6, to a total of 2 per Skill. Furthermore, in tests of Resilience or when Foraging and Hunting in their natural habitats, Paltris characters can re-roll a number of 1s equal to their Will exp once per session, embodying their ability to adapt and persevere in the face of adversity.

# Inspiration Concept:

The Paltris draw inspiration from the resourcefulness and resilience exhibited by individuals of Spanish and North African descent. Their cultural identity encompasses the strength and adaptability found within these regions, reflecting a combination of rugged determination and the ability to thrive in challenging environments. The Paltris embody the spirit of self-reliance, forging their own path and finding success where others may falter. Their connection to the Star Gods infuses them with a profound sense of spirituality and enlightenment, making them natural leaders in matters of faith and devotion.

Overall, the Paltris stand as a race of resilient and self-reliant individuals, deeply connected to the Star Gods and guided by their light. They possess an innate ability to thrive in the most challenging and remote regions, their dusky skin reflecting their resilience against environmental hardships. With their inherent hardiness and adaptability, the Paltris forge their own destiny, valuing personal agency and embracing their independent spirit while finding solace and strength in their unwavering connection to the celestial realms.
