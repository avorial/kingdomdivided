# The First Bellringer Crusade (The Fall of Kings)

## Goals

The Bellringer Missionaries conquering of the Lands of Earth and Water, Known today as the Bell Lands.

## Dates

* Start Date: 347 S.E.

* End Date: 221 S.E.

* Length 126 years

## Combatants

### Bellringers

- [[House-Graisti|House Graisti]]
- [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]]
- [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] (A minor house)
- [[House-Montmier|House Montmier]] (In 262 S.E)
- House Sedi (In 247 S.E.)
- [[House-Whitestar|House Whitestar]] (In 240 S.E.)
- Allied House Almirn

### Esten [[Esten-Kings|Kings]]

- [[Esten-Kings|House Oquil]]
- [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]]
- [[Esten-Kings|House Benoman]]
- [[house_talmart|House Talmert]](In 235 S.E. they become Neutral, and Leave the war)

## The Pact of Wood for Land

This is the pact made with the Saltblood Captains (Later organized to House Almirn), to transport any and all Bellringer Crusaders for vast tracks' of land after the First Crusade was over.

## The Landing

The first landing was strategically planned at Requena, As it was the most defensible location on the coast. [[House-Graisti|House Graisti]] built they key defenses at Belwall, As [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]] & [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] troops advance they are attacked strongly by [[Esten-Kings|House Oquil]] griffon riders forcing a retreat back to Bellwall. [[House-Graisti|House Graisti]] bests [[Esten-Kings|House Oquil]] and the first major attack goes to the Crusaders.

## Advance to the North

After reinforcements land [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]] sneaks troops across the desert wastes of the west. Traveling only at night, and burying their tents during the day they arrive at Coldwater and successfully attack the unaware city taking it from House Benoman. Using captured ships, the Saltblood Captains are able to reinforce them by the time Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]] Units arrive. Now having 2 major ports the Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]] retreat to beyond the mountains.

## The Night of Feathers and Blood

Having captured many of [[Esten-Kings|House Oquil]]'s Griffons, [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] spends the next 10 years training to use them. Using [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]]'s Tactics of pushing as far as they could and holding it the goal was to strike right at the capital at Oldwyn. They did not make it that far though, while passing though Rivercliff and Arta they were attacked by House Minimar troops. They fought valiantly but were forced to retreat to Rivercliff and defend from there.

## The Moons of Sorrow

The Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]] fought strongly, having many wins but losing too many numbers. They finally routed House Rivers from Rivercliff and felt they had them on the route, and they committed the majority of [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]] troops to finally destroy [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] once and for all, they had them surrounded on a full moon night. While [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] parlayed to [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]] they did not realize that they were being surrounded by [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]] reinforcements. While [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]] now was on the losing side, they let [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] leave waiting to hear the terms given by [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]]. But as soon as [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] was safe, [[House-Covinggard|House Covinggard]] slaughtered [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]] to the man.

## The Fall of Oldwyn

After the Moons of Sorrow, and the fall of [[Esten-Kings|House Minimar]] Oldwyn was left underdefended. The siege only lasted 45 days until [[House-Rivers|House Rivers]] took it. Over the next 60 years multiple attacks landed by the Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]] but they never retook Oldwyn.

## The Knights of The Scarlett

The Knights of Sir Valen ([[House-Montmier|House Montmier]]) arrive on Saltblood ships, Striking from the west they make landfall and take the city of Wickham, Gradually Moving Up to Valhurst. These wars push [[Esten-Kings|House Benoman]] to surrender. They surrender their arms but are allowed to live.

## The Rise of House Sedi

Ethan Dukor falls in the Great Well, and is lost for a full lunar month. When he arose from the well he said that he had conquered the darkness. His changed state gave him an ease for magical powers and the ability to pass it down his bloodline. He then founded House Sedi and though his inspiration the second great push of the war starts.

## The Starfire Baptism

In 240 S.E. Ethan Sedi, had a council with the Ferdor Tribes to enlist them in the wars vs the Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]]. He said that he had been told by the Bellringer that the children of Angus were a blessed people. If they called out to the Bellringer he would answer. He gave them a secret cantillation of the Bellringers name. Their Chiefin Uthar made a deal with Ethan, if his people converted to the Bellringer they would aid him. If they did not, they would aid the Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]] to destroy the Bellringers. Ethan took the deal, Uthar spoke the words, as the stars shined as he spoke, responding to the sounds. All of the now "[[House-Whitestar|House Whitestar]]" converted to the Bellringer faith and joined House Sedi's armies.

## The Pact of Wood and Tears

In 235 S.E. Ethan Sedi Met with [[house_talmart|House Talmert]] diplomats offering a pact. Their people would be spared of the war, if they did not fight any longer on the Esten [[Bellringer-Crusades|Kings]]'s side. [[house_talmart|House Talmert]], leary of trusting Ethan Sedi after the slauring done by other Crusader troops, said they would agree if his newborn son and heir Athos Sedi would be held hostage. Ethan Sedi agreed and as the paper was signed it was said that young Athos Sedi's crys could be heard all though Crystalwood.

## The Fall of the High King

House Sedi Troops push the High King out of Mullrose towards Handfasted. Legend says that they were running to a vital lay line that would let them empower a spell that would turn the tide of the war. But before the ritual was finished Hose Sedi troops captured the High King and forced him to abdicate his throne to Ethan Sedi. His children were allowed to live, if they converted to the Bellringer faith. Historians debate if this was the end of the First Crusade or if it ends 3 years later as Ethan Sedi is crowned the first Bellringer Emperor.

# The Second Bellringer Crusade (The Peals Hersey)

## Goals

Orthodox Bellringers fighting to purge the Peals Hersey from the land.

## Dates

- Start Date 157 S.E.
- End Date 96 S.E.
- Length 61 Years

## Combatants

### Pellists

Montrose Sedi and his Pealist Forces

### Bellringer Orthodoxy

Grand Hierophant Watson and his Orthodox Bell Ringer Forces

## The Split of the Church

Since the rise of House Sedi to the Imperial seat, the cause of the Church of the Bellringers has waived. The simple faith of the common man and noble, was a very orthodox teaching of the Light of the Bellringer. Those that were more learned or seekers felt called to another teaching, a teaching known as the Peals. The Peals felt that the Bell Ringer was not the stars above, but the darkness between them as a man covered by a bell is in darkness so is the true message of the Bellringer. In 157 S.E. The schism reached a fevered pitch as the Grand Hierophant declared all Peals heretics and that any who gave them succor would be burned alive.

## The Silence of the Bells

Historians often wonder why outright conflict did not start right away. Many at the time felt that the Grand Hierophant's ruling would mostly be ignored, others who were wiser merely knew that this was time to prepare. For close to a decade, there were fights but no great battles.

## The Screams of Faith

In 141 S.E. the first major act of the war happened. The Grand Hierophant declared that Montrose Sedi, the Heir of the House Sedi was to be burned. There was no doubt he was a Pealist, but still his father tried to convince the Grand Hierophant to be lenient, but as heresy was growing in the country an example had to be made. As he was to be taken to the pyre a group of Pealist monks ambushed the guards and fled Oldwyn. The time for silence was over, Brother against brother, house against house, the war flared up on both sides of the Bell Lands.

## Fires of Darkness

In 148 SE Montrose found by a group of bellringers in a barn, the barn is burned down as he becomes a martyr to the Pellists.

## The Last Stand

In 110 SE The last formal Pellist army in destroyed on the field after that it is just ambush raids and the church burning those they feel were supporters.

## The End of the war

As Grand Hierophant Watson is turning ill, in his 90th year he calls an end to the Crusade, feeling that it has been turned in to a political tool.

# The Third Bellringer Crusade (The Mikado Invasion)

## Goals

## Dates

- End Date None

- Bellringer Crusader Armies

### Mikado Imperial Armies

- Mikado Home Guard

## The Fate of the Seas

The Great Bellringer War or just the "Great War" is the common name for the expansion of aggressions against the Mikado Empire. Though this was gradually formed into it's own arm of the Imperial Army.

These units are formally called 'Zealots' but they themselves will commonly call themselves crusaders. This brings a confusion that is ignored by many. Crusaders are troops led by a member of the Clapper order of the Bell Ringer. Clappers are commonly leaders or advisors to Zealot troops but not always. It is not an insult to make this mistake so correct usage is normally only cared about by Tested scholars.

Men & Women who succeed in a tour of duty of 10 years in the war are automatically awarded the title of Sir/Lady regardless of their backgrounds. Also it is presumed any crime, religious or secular is forgiven after 10 years in the Great War.

## Key Locations of the Great War

After the successes of House Bellkind to cleanse the land, they realized they would need a more stable means to get troops to the Mikado. With the aid of King of the Bells Ustof Minor he made a treaty with the King of Worsouk To allow him to land troops there. Under the Bellringer seal it was declared that not one foot of Worsouk domain would be claimed. This became a symbiotic relationship, troops would land heavy with gold they would spend in this domain.

They try not to think about it too deeply that Worsouk can't supply enough food for the Bellringer troops, they are using that gold to by food from the Mikado to feed the Bellringers. While the unified might of Gratsi and the Imperial Army set sail, legends say that the sunset on that night was crimson red. Before even the first port, a massive hurricane rose and destroyed the fleets. Less then 1 man in a hundred lived just holding on to wreckage. Even this though was not the end of the Bellringers troubles. With prophetic timing the Mikado sailed right after the hurricane left their shores. Landing at Bedford, and dropping off troops.

While the Crusades of the Bell Ringers, have normally been successful, the 3rd Crusade was a major failure. After the the destruction of the Imperial navy, the south was invaded by the forces of Mikado Orin Toab. The Mikado's troops made landfall and pushed up to the Talmert border. Held back by their bows, the for the first time in 200 years the land was held by a heretic.

Emperor Yanick Rivers called upon the nobles of the land offering every mile regained in the Bell Ringers name would gain those lands. Many tried, and many died. Until a Bell Ringer Priest rose, and inspired the people. Lesser nobles from all the lands joined Enzio Bellkind in his reconquista.

It took 15 years, but the Mikado forces were pushed to the sea. The House Toab was never eliminated though. Generations pass and new armies are formed to keep the Mikado at bay, but as time as progressed. Few outside of the Bell Kind lands find a foreign threat to be worthy of much concern.
