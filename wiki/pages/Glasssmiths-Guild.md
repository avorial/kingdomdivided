# The **Glasssmith's** of the Leandan League

**Guildmaster:** Farid Al-Maghribi (M) (Paltrist)

**Minor Guilds: **Glassware and Lensers

---

The Glasssmith's Guild stands as the master craftsmen of Leandan, wielding the transformative power of fire and sand to shape ordinary materials into exquisite works of art. With skilled hands and meticulous precision, they take coarse white sands and imbue them with beauty and utility, producing fine panes of glass that adorn windows and illuminate interiors with natural light.

But their artistry doesn't end there; within their ranks, the minor guild of Lensers elevates their craft to new heights. These skilled artisans refine their glass to create specialized lenses for a variety of purposes, from the spyglasses used by ship captains to the delicate scopes employed by Physicians. With a commitment to perfection and an unwavering dedication to their craft, the Glasssmith's Guild ensures that Leandan's vision is always crystal clear, both literally and metaphorically.
