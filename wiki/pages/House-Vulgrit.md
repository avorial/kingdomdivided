# History

## Legend of Kharazan Vulgrit: Conqueror of Redgate

In the ancient annals of House Vulgrit, there lies a legendary tale of their founder, Kharazan Vulgrit, a mighty warrior who led his people with unwavering courage and determination during the tumultuous era of the Bellringer Crusade. Born amidst the sweeping plains and rugged mountains of the Zafroff region, Kharazan was destined to leave an indelible mark on the land and carve out a prosperous future for his people.

According to the ancient sagas, Kharazan was a charismatic and skilled warlord, revered for his unparalleled military prowess and strategic brilliance. The Esten people, a formidable tribe that had long held dominion over the lands surrounding the city now known as Redgate, posed a formidable challenge to Kharazan and his fledgling House Vulgrit. Determined to secure a prosperous future for his people, Kharazan rallied his warriors, united neighboring tribes under his banner, and embarked on a daring campaign to reclaim their ancestral lands.

The legend speaks of countless battles fought against the Esten forces, where Kharazan led his troops with unyielding resolve, displaying remarkable feats of valor and tactical brilliance. With each victory, his reputation as a fearsome warrior and visionary leader grew, and his influence expanded across the region. His strategic genius allowed him to outmaneuver his adversaries, employing unconventional tactics that left the Esten bewildered and in disarray.

The turning point came when Kharazan led a daring assault on the city that would come to be known as Redgate. The Esten defenders, caught off guard by the audacity and ferocity of House Vulgrit's attack, found themselves outmatched and outmaneuvered. Kharazan's forces breached the city walls, their battle cries echoing through the streets as they swept through the defenders like a tempest, bringing the city to its knees.

It is said that Kharazan stood atop the highest tower of Redgate, surveying the conquered city with a mix of pride and determination. The banners of House Vulgrit were raised high, bearing the symbol of a golden hand, a testament to their triumphant victory. From that moment, Redgate became the seat of House Vulgrit's power, their stronghold within the land they had wrested from the Esten people.

The legend of Kharazan Vulgrit's conquest and the founding of Redgate spread far and wide, inspiring generations of House Vulgrit's descendants. His name became synonymous with courage, resilience, and unyielding determination in the face of adversity. The tale of his heroic deeds, passed down through the ages, serves as a reminder of the indomitable spirit and enduring legacy of House Vulgrit.

To this day, the city of Redgate stands as a testament to the vision and tenacity of Kharazan Vulgrit, a lasting tribute to the house's founder and the enduring spirit of their ancestors.

## Current Times

House Vulgrit, a banner to House Covenguard, holds its dominion over the fortress city of Redgate. Nestled along the great canal and near the serene Lake Minik, their ancestral lands are defined by a rugged landscape. To the south, a formidable mountain range looms, while the northwestern expanse unfolds into vast plains reminiscent of the untamed steppe. It is within this untamed realm that House Vulgrit thrives, forging a distinct identity that reflects the spirit of the people they interact with.

Rooted in a culture that carries echoes of the ancient Zafroff, House Vulgrit stands as a bridge between the settled nobility and the nomadic tribes that roam the land. They have with a shared reverence for nature, equestrian prowess, and a strong warrior tradition. As one of the first banners to align with House Covenguard during the Belllands' Crusader Wars, House Vulgrit has a proud history of valorous deeds and unwavering loyalty.

Leading House Vulgrit are Baron Rey Vulgrit and Baroness Donnia Vulgrit, wise and astute rulers who navigate the complex tapestry of politics and tribal dynamics. They oversee the governance of Redgate, fostering prosperity and maintaining harmony within their domain. Their leadership has earned the respect and allegiance of their vassals and chieftains, ensuring a delicate balance between tradition and progress.

Sir Maxim Vulgrit, the eldest son and heir to House Vulgrit, embodies the spirit of his people. A skilled horseman and fierce warrior, he commands House Vulgrit's forces, safeguarding the domain against external threats. Sir Maxim's martial prowess and strategic acumen make him a formidable presence on the battlefield, earning him admiration from both allies and rivals alike.

Lady Nastasiya Vulgrit, the only daughter of House Vulgrit, possesses a deep connection to the early Bellringer faith. Rooted in the ancient traditions of the land, she embraces her role as a native mystic. Lady Nastasiya has spent years studying the rituals and practices of the early Bellringer faith, delving into its mysteries and unlocking hidden wisdom. Her spiritual insights and mystical abilities grant her a unique perspective, allowing her to serve as a guide to House Vulgrit and its vassals, as well as a beacon of hope to those seeking solace and enlightenment.

Completing the family is Sir Sergiy Vulgrit, the second son of House Vulgrit. As a scholar and historian, Sir Sergiy delves deep into the ancient teachings of the early Bellringer faith. His extensive knowledge of the rituals, scriptures, and sacred practices makes him a learned guide not only to House Vulgrit but also to the tribal communities who seek wisdom and understanding. Sir Sergiy's passion for uncovering forgotten lore and preserving the rich cultural heritage of the Belllands is an invaluable asset to House Vulgrit.

As House Vulgrit interactions with people nearby, their influence extends beyond political and military matters. They act as a stabilizing force, respecting and preserving the ancestral customs and rituals of the land. House Vulgrit's leaders, including Sir Sergiy, actively foster relationships with tribal leaders, working toward mutual understanding and cooperation. Through their cultural exchanges, House Vulgrit becomes a beacon of unity, embracing the diversity and strength that the tribal traditions bring to the region.

House Vulgrit's influence may be less extensive compared to other banners, but their close connections to the tribal people and their commitment to preserving the early Bellringer faith make them a valued presence in the Belllands. Their domain of Redgate stands as a testament to their resilience and their ability to harmoniously unite different cultures and traditions.
