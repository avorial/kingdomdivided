# Athletics

## Acrobatics, Balance, Contortions, Dodge, Quickness

* on these I could only see the most extreme things needing the specialty. Like you are trying to get in a hole smaller then your shoulders ect.

# Animal Handing

## Charm, Drive, Ride, Train

* Very simple tasks might not take a skill, riding a pony with a person holding a rope, or feeding a dog to follow you.. but anything more complicated would take a specialty.

# Athletics

## Climb, Jump, Run, Strength, Swim, Throw

* Very simple tasks might not take a skill, even swimming in a small pond might be ok without swimming.. but a ocean or a flowing river yes.

# Awareness

## Empathy, Notice

* Only rarely will this take a skill if the thing is very well hidden.

# Cunning

## Decipher, Logic, Memory

* Only rarely will this take a skill

# Deception

## Act, Bluff, Cheat, Disguise

* Only rarely will this take a skill, disguise being the outlier.. if you don't have it you don't have the ability to look like another person.

# Endurance

## Resilience, Stamina

* Only rarely will this take a skill

# Fighting

## Axes, Bludgeons, Brawling, Fencing, Long Blades, Pole-Arms, Short Blades, Spears

* This has it's own mechanic on "training" with weapons.

# Healing

## Diagnose, Treat Ailment, Treat Injury

* This is VITAL for any use.

# Language

* This is VITAL for any use.

*Every other language is a speciality to have at 1 less then your natural skill.*

*So if you have linguists 3 and you buy another language as a specialty you can speak in it and understand but you have trouble writing it.*

*There are 2 listed language speciates. Eloquence, and "literacy" I would consider the first letting you have the abilities to try to do intrigues though writing, and the other is you have the ability to write and chronicle large amounts of information such as a novel or a science book.*

# Knowledge

## Education, Research, Streetwise

* This is heavily preferred for anything but common knowledge's.

# Marksmanship

## Bows, Crossbows, Siege, Thrown

* This has it's own mechanic on "training" with weapons.

# Persuasion

## Bargain, Charm, Convince, Incite, Intimidate, Seduce, Taunt

* This is preferred (but not always needed) if you want to use that type of persuasion.

# Status

* Very simple tasks might not take a skill, but most will.

# Thievery

## Pick Lock, Sleight of Hand, Steal

# Warfare/Warcraft

## Command, Strategy, Tactics

* Very simple tasks might not take a skill, but most will.

# Will
