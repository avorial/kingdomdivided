History:

House Ulbrecht, is a of branch of [[House-Henge|House Henge]], as [[House-Henge|House Henge]] they have a history going back centuries, as House Ulbrecht merely months..

[[siobhán_silverridge|Siobhán Silverridge]]’s rise to the Duchess of Whitestar was the origin point in the history of House Ulbrecht. In the deals and trades that were made to get her to her title, her family’s ancestral home was now in a domain that she was not part of. Wanting to make the transition as easy as possible, they allowed her cousin to take a new last name and be the knight/mayor of their old town of Faymill. He now rules over a “new domain” that was his family’s for generations.   
  
The people feel left in the cold and are waiting to see if the poor times are at an end. But they trust Amund and hope that he can pull together a collation of the local families to make the town strong again.
