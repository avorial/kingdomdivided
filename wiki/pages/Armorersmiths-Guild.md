The Armorersmith Guild stands for its craftsmanship, renowned across Leandan for their mastery of scale, chain, split, and plate armor. Their skill in forging protective gear is unmatched, providing warriors with armor that combines both durability and elegance. However, their expertise extends beyond traditional armaments.

Within the hallowed halls of the guild, they nurture the burgeoning field of Automatons, delving into the realm of mechanical marvels. Here, the Minor Guild Houses of Machinists and Automation find their home, where they collaborate to push the boundaries of innovation. Automation, the art of crafting and refining robotics, is a discipline embraced and honed within the guild's walls, driving progress and ushering in a new era of technological advancement.
