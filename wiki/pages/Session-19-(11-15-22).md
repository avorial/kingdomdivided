## Arrival at Greenmarble

As they arrive the Rainbow Knights greet them, saying they have been sent from Duchess Oliva Pearlstone to escort them though Pearstone to Mistwick mostly to make sure their troops don't harass the countryside. The party (except for Shivan) camp outside of the city.

They meet the Mayor Boniface Ferguson who brings food and entertainment as requested Carrick Silverridge. A few moments later the Mayor returns with a man who plays a tune, and then leaves.. To then "return?" with another man. The party realizes that the "mayor" had been faked. A notice was left by the band known as Silent Devils who's talents go to spying and sneaking in to keeps. The players higher them for 500 gold, with 100 as a downpayment to rescue the girl from Ganymede.
