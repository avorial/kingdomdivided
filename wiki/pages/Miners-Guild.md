# The **Miners **Guild of the Leandan League

**Guildmaster:** Cornelis van der Zwaan (M) (Welvart)

**Minor Guilds:** Masons, Stonecutters

---

The Leandan Miners represent the backbone of the nation, tirelessly extracting the coveted Crystals from the depths of the earth. As the workhorses of the realm, they embody steadfastness and resilience, their dedication unwavering as they toil in the darkness of the mines.

Often characterized by their soft-spoken demeanor, mirroring that of their leader, the miners exemplify humility and diligence in their craft. Alongside them, Masons and Stonecutters play integral roles within the mining industry. While both professions involve working with stone, Masons focus on building structures, utilizing stone, bricks, and other materials to construct walls, buildings, and other architectural features. On the other hand, Stonecutters specialize in shaping and carving stone into specific shapes and designs, often for decorative or ornamental purposes.

Together, Miners, Masons, and Stonecutters collaborate seamlessly with multiple industries, ensuring that Leandan receives nothing but the finest craftsmanship and resources. Their collective efforts contribute significantly to the prosperity and advancement of the nation.
