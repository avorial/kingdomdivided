## Star Gods

Star gods are those who are formed from the tears of O, during the earliest days of this creation. They are known for having deeper knowledge but strong aspects of paranoia and jealousy.

## Deities

### Kilat

The god of winds and thunder, followed by many outside the Bell Lands. Recently, with the rise of the Saffron Society, followers of Kilat have been getting more and more followers in the Bell Lands.

*The Weather God — Kilat*

## Old Gods

Old gods are those who were devoured by O during the last creation. While they were being eaten they lost many of their memories, but still kept their unique powers from the previous creation. They are very close to their followers, normally with some avatar in the world.
