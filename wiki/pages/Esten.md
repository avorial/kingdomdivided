## **Rituals of the Esten**

### **The Esten Joining**

The Esten Joining is a profound and mystical rite of passage for children of the Esten people, held on the night of the Esten New Year. This sacred ceremony takes place when the two celestial spheres align in the night sky, an event believed to strengthen the spiritual connection between the mortal realm and the old gods. Children, usually around nine or ten years old, participate in this ritual to discover their spiritual alignment and future within the Esten society.

#### **The Ritual**

On the night of the Joining, families gather at sacred sites under the aligned spheres. These sites are often ancient ruins, groves, or stone circles, each steeped in the history and power of the Esten Kings. The air is filled with anticipation and reverence as the community comes together to witness the next generation's spiritual awakening.

**The Task:**

Each child is given a minor yet significant task that is believed to invoke the presence of the old gods. These tasks vary depending on local traditions and the child's heritage but often involve elements of nature, magic, and ancestral homage. Examples include:

- **Lighting a Sacred Flame:** A child might be tasked with kindling a fire from a single spark, symbolizing the ignition of their inner light and potential.
- **Drawing Water from a Hidden Spring:** This task represents the uncovering of hidden truths and the flow of life and wisdom.
- **Weaving a Protective Talisman:** Using natural materials, the child weaves a talisman to symbolize their connection to the world and the protection of the old gods.

As the children complete their tasks, a magical event occurs, signifying their spiritual connection to the old gods. This event varies for each child, reflecting their unique essence and potential. It might be a sudden gust of wind, a spontaneous blooming of flowers, or an ethereal glow enveloping the child.

#### **The Vision**

Typically, the old gods guide the child to the House of their parents, affirming their familial and cultural heritage. However, the gods are known to be unpredictable, and about 20% of the time, they grant the child a vision compelling them to join a different Esten house. These visions are vivid and undeniable, filled with symbols and messages that resonate deeply with the child's soul.

In these visions, the child might see themselves performing significant acts within another house, encountering ancestors from different lineages, or receiving blessings from the gods associated with that house. These visions are transformative, often changing the child's path and identity.

#### **Consequences and Choices**

While the visions are powerful, some children choose not to reveal them, preferring to stay with their parents and within their familiar environment. This decision is respected, but it leaves an indelible mark on the child's spirit. Those who embrace the vision and join another house often undergo further rites to integrate them into their new community, receiving guidance and training to fulfill their new roles.

The Joining not only determines the child's future but also reinforces the bonds between the Esten houses, as each child's journey reflects the interconnectedness of their society. The ceremony is a reminder of the ever-present influence of the old gods and the enduring legacy of the Esten Kings.

In summary, the Esten Joining is a pivotal and magical event that shapes the identity and destiny of the Esten children. Through their tasks and the ensuing spiritual revelations, they discover their place within the rich tapestry of Esten heritage, guided by the wisdom and whims of the old gods.
