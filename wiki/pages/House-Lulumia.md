## [¶](#house-lulumia) House Lulumia

**Title:** The Velvet Knives  
**Location:** Red Terrace District, Court of Xinjing  
**Faith:** Esten Ancestor Worship (infused with Worm God legal doctrine)  
**Role:** Political manipulators, cultural preservationists, imperial advisors, Esten representation in law and policy

### [¶](#origins-and-legacy) Origins and Legacy

House Lulumia descends from Esten nobility who fled during the Sedi invasion. In exile, they buried their names in etiquette and law, surviving through mastery of the Mikado court system. Their influence lies in bureaucracy, ritual, and behind-the-scenes manipulation. They are one of the two surviving Esten noble houses in the Mikado Empire.

### [¶](#cultural-identity) Cultural Identity

Adorn quillfish-embroidered robes (symbol of silent precision)

Speak in fossilized Old Esten dialects

Custodians of the Mirror Archive (a record of ancestral claims and oaths)

### [¶](#relationship-with-returning-esten) Relationship with Returning Esten

The arrival of Sedi-Esten immigrants from Talmart—many seeking reconnection with lost houses—has strained Lulumia's political and cultural position. While some in Lulumia wish to restore lost bloodlines, others see the newcomers as dangerous destabilizers.

### [¶](#political-status) Political Status

Lulumia holds sway in legal and religious circles, but faces increasing skepticism from both Mikado elites and Esten radicals. Their resistance to immediate recognition of immigrant bloodlines is perceived by some as betrayal.
