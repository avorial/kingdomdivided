## [¶](#house-mikado) **House Mikado**

**Title:** The Imperial House  
**Symbol:** The Golden Salamander on a Field of Crimson  
**Location:** The Celestial Palace, Capital City of Yazhouyun  
**Faith:** Worm Gods  
**Role:** Divine Rulers, Keepers of the Pact

### [¶](#overview) **Overview**

House Mikado is not merely a ruling dynasty—it is a divine lineage woven into the very fabric of the world. Descended from the first human to be reshaped by the Worm Gods, the Mikado bloodline is considered sacred. They are the embodiment of the Empire’s founding mythos and the living representation of unity between mortal kind and the divine.

They rarely appear in public, cloaked in mystery and myth, and when they do, their presence is heralded as a celestial event. Despite their distance from daily politics, their every word ripples through the empire like prophecy. Their role is to guide, not to govern. They interpret the will of the Worm Gods and make final decrees that shape the direction of the entire realm.

### [¶](#leadership) **Leadership**

![de29096b18b2f64cdedfb6d5543eaeba](/wiki/media/de29096b18b2f64cdedfb6d5543eaeba.jpg)
Emperor Mikado Xelin IV

**Emperor Mikado Xelin IV**  
The current Mikado Emperor, Xelin IV, is in his early thirties but is already viewed as one of the most enigmatic figures in the Empire’s history. Known for his distant gaze and cryptic speeches, he speaks rarely, and only in poetic riddles interpreted by the priests of House Maj. Rumors persist that Xelin IV can no longer touch the earth directly and floats inches above the ground at all times. His physical form is a Raven, that shimmers red under the under certain moon phases—a sign that he is being further transformed by the Worm Gods.

**Imperial Consort Lian of Jeong**  
A brilliant strategist and diplomat, Lian was chosen not through arranged marriage but by the Emperor himself—breaking centuries of tradition. Her influence has modernized many aspects of court protocol and diplomacy. She is a balancing force to the Emperor’s otherworldliness.

### [¶](#key-members) **Key Members**

**High Priestess Taiyu (House Maj, assigned to the Mikado Court)**  
Though not of Mikado blood, Taiyu is considered the voice of the gods and interprets the Emperor’s will. She is deeply feared and respected—when she speaks, the entire palace falls silent. Her white mask has never been removed in public.

**Prince Mikado Shenren ("The Quiet Prince")**  
The younger brother of Xelin IV, Shenren is the only Mikado known to have traveled beyond the capital. Quiet and philosophical, he was once seen speaking with soldiers and commoners alike. Some believe he is being groomed to lead the Empire in times of war, should the Ai Hoku fail.

**Archivist Zhenha Mikado**  
Keeper of the Imperial Archives and rumored to be hundreds of years old, Zhenha claims to remember the voices of emperors past. A strange figure, she is rarely seen, but her influence over historical narrative and legitimacy is enormous.
