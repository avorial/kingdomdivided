# The **Smiths **Guild of the Leandan League

**Guildmaster: **Elise Dubois (F) (Quintill)

**Minor Guilds:** Finesmiths, Locksmiths

---

While the Armorsmiths and Weaponsmiths have branched off to specialize in their respective fields, the Smiths of the Leandan League remain the versatile artisans, adept at shaping metal into a myriad of forms, all everyday but never commonplace. From crafting intricate silver platters to fashioning lush door knobs, their skill knows no bounds. Whether it's a delicate golden inlay or robust locks to secure treasured possessions, the Smiths pride themselves on their meticulous workmanship. Within their guild, the minor branches of Finesmiths and Locksmiths further hone their craft, focusing on the finer details of metalwork and the art of locksmithing respectively. Together, the Leandan Smiths continue to uphold the tradition of excellence in metal craftsmanship, ensuring that every piece they forge is a testament to their skill and dedication.
