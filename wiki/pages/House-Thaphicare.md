## [¶](#house-thaphicarea) House Thaphicarea

**Title:** The Fire-Bearers  
**Location:** Rural and coastal Lulumia  
**Faith:** Pure-form Esten Ancestral Worship, emphasis on ritual fire and prophecy  
**Role:** Cultural guardians, rural communal leaders, spiritual revivalists

### [¶](#origins-and-legacy) Origins and Legacy

Thaphicarea fled during the Sedi conquest and retained more of their traditional Esten identity than Lulumia. They settled in rural regions of the Mikado Empire, fostering community cohesion, rituals, and ancestor veneration without bending to imperial court norms.

### [¶](#cultural-identity) Cultural Identity

Maintain open flame temples

Practice Angalaglassi fire rites in secret

Conduct seasonal ancestral firewalks

### [¶](#relationship-with-returning-esten) Relationship with Returning Esten

Unlike Lulumia, House Thaphicarea has embraced the new arrivals—especially those who bring old rituals and long-lost lore. Their lands have become centers of Esten communal life and renewal.

### [¶](#political-status) Political Status

Though lacking formal power in the court, Thaphicarea commands growing moral and spiritual influence among both Sedi-Esten and native Esten descendants. They are now viewed as a possible alternative center of Esten identity within the empire.

**Matriarch Kael Thaphicare**  
A strong, unbending elder who wears robes dyed in earth-pigment and carries a ceremonial staff of sunflower stalks. Kael rules not by decree but by collective memory. She speaks in parables and decisions are made through communal vote.

**Key Members**

**Heir Luthin Thaphicare**  
Kael’s grandson, a fierce young farmer-general who trains militia forces in case of crop raids or rebellion. Charismatic and idealistic, Luthin seeks to give the Esten people their own voice in the Empire.

**Scribe-Mother Eresi Thaphicare**  
A historian and keeper of the Esten oral tradition. She records every family’s ancestral lines and public oaths. No wedding or festival in the plains happens without her blessing.

**Harvest-Sister Mali Thaphicare**  
A spiritual leader who blesses the fields and channels fertility magic from the Worm Gods. Her rituals are seen as critical for the health of the realm’s crops.
