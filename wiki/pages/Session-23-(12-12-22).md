# Events

## The Mist brings a old family member

After the attack, the cadre leaves the camp to investigate other attackers, before they can leave. Casimir Ramirez leaves his ship coming from the crusades, as he finds the encampment of House Silverridge and then is taken before Connor Silverrige after a bit of conversation they realize that he is one of Connor Silverrige's children.

After a bit of conversation he joins with the party to investigate the area attacked.

## Attack on the King of Bells

They see groups of troops going to the King of Bells Endulaudaus, as they arrive they see that he has been attacked with of the attackers killed and another escaped. After some investigation they seem to think that the killers are likely House Tarasovich's Shadow walkers.

They also notice that the tested know, that one of the houses in attendance are likely House Sedi Supporters, and thus must be stopped.

## Meeting with the Pale Walkers

After that the party goes to House Talmert's encampment and meet with Dutchess Mercy Talmert she offers to work with them, if the offer is good but the players do not offer her anything yet. They are keeping their options open at least until troops march.

Casimir Made friends with a Talmart Guard Alexi

## Wedding?

Carrick Silverridge goes to talk to Lady Myo Songprawati and after some small talk (where she notes the Saffron's have stolen 5 dragons) she says that her mother is asking her to meet other suitor's, Carrick Silverridge is unwilling to give a commitment. She says she will tell him before she actually marries anyone.

## Meeting with the Last of the Houses

### House Whitestar House Ceanadch

They meet with Duke Timothy White-Star and socially deal well with him and then he brings up that one of his banners (Setanta Ceanadach) has a desire to marry Ingrid Henge, if this is matched by the parents, he will pay off the debts of House Henge.

Also he writes that Fiona Silveroak is requesting to know if Caden Maddix has any interest in her.

A old war friend Quintin talks to Casimir Ramirez and they reestablish their relationship.

### House Graisti House Lombardo

As they had met, Peacekeeper Noel Lombardo she greets them and has them meet Sir Matteo Lombardo who even though with his low title, tries his best to be supportive of the party.

Duke Enzo Graisti meets with them giving Carrick Silverridge an imperial saber from the House Sedi's 5th Emperor. The Sword of Athos. He offers to have the cadre come to a more private dinner, that the players go to with the next game starting.
