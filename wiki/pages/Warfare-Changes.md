# Generals and Subcommanders

A General is the leader of one side of the warfare combat. Their stats will be rolled for the Initiative. They can have a number of sub commanders equal to their status (if noble, or Will if commoner), but all sub commanders can issue a full amount of actions equal to their warfare skill.

# Starting Unit Equipment

For ease of mechanic, units are equipped like a normal member of that unit, when in warfare that unit gets the benefits of being a unit. This allows you to pull off any member of the unit and say what they have, and also upgrade them all.

## Special Unit Type

Special Units that take Will as one of their 3 starting bonus abilities are expected to have some magical trait or spell.

## Cadre Benefit

Cadre no longer has a requirement before buying it. If you do not have the requirement though they will not take your orders. This is meant to simulate a person who has bodyguards but is not a person who is a commander by themselves.

A cadre starts as a Veteran Class of any Unit but Warship. They should be the type of unit that makes sense in the history of the purchase not just what is the most expensive troop choice. They can be upgraded with power, and normal training rolls. If they are ever totally destroyed a new unit will form how long it takes depends on how the first unit was treated. A mistreated unit always sent to the front will take almost 30 sessions to reform, but your beloved group that was lost from a bad roll will return shortly.

## Unique Units

- Peasant Believers (A Peasant Levies unit that is easier to command due to the fact of their fanatical faith in a cause)
- War Beetles (A Special Calvary Unit)
- Dragon Riders (A riding unit, imagine a large komto dragon)

# Unit Experience and Growth

Experience is spent like it was a normal character other then the aspect they can not buy destiny or benefits without the storytellers express permission. (10 for specialties, 30 for attributes)

- Green [+1 Ability] [or 30 xp]
- Trained [+3 Abilities] [or 90 xp]
- Veteran [+5 Abilities] [or 150 xp] Can have 1 skill at 4
- Elite [+7 Abilities] [or 210 xp] Can have 2 skills at 4
- Every rank gained above elite gives 30 xp and their skill cap is +1 (the first upgrade to 5)

# Unit Equipment

A unit will have all the same gear unless upgraded for the silver cost of that gear x10. This also allows you to get the benefit of higher class gear such as castle forged or the like. If a unit is destroyed on the field of battle, or your own unit dies in a bad training roll, half of their gear is recoverable. Over time that might upgrade your own units.
