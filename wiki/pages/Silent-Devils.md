# History

Formed from a group of actors, who was in a city that was being attacked. Not seeing a way to flee they put on costumes that made them look like the attackers. They were able to sneak among them and kill each of them without getting caught. Realizing that this would be a more profitable way to conduct business they took the attackers weapons and horses and founded the Silent Devils.

Over the years they became renowned for getting in to defensible buildings and attacking from within. They were going to be declared outlaws and their charter removed by House Rivers But the Pearstone nobility stood for them, in a famous speech saying "The charter of the Mercenary Bands, allows them to fight with weapons in all lands who are we to say what is a weapon?". The debate won the day and the Silent Devils kept plying their trade.
