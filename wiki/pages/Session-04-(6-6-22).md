# Events

Having just liberated Deerholt They notice a bit of smoke to the north in House Whitestar domain, traveling there they come across a group of imperial troops of House Sherman. They don't have a great interaction with High General Douglas Sherman, but he shows them that he is hunting the Saffron Society leader Samson Kreeg. They depart and return to Windbreak telling the court what happened, to keep Carrick Silverridge safe they decide to deal with the ruins above Windbreak.

In arriving there they find a sacrifice pit, and a mosaic that describes dark rituals of rebirth. They go deeper and find 3 books.

*A book of the dead, having the names of the sacrificed.

*A book of life, the names of those who have been reborn.

The Diary of Ruisín Ó Duinn

They return after some time dilatations and fleeing from the cultists. They tell the court, and Conner Silverridge say he will call Bellringer priests to cleans the site.
