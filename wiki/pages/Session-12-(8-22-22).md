# Events

The adventure started as The Cadre on the outskirts of Faemill, with Leon Blackwood after they arrive he stays at a local in waiting for the information they promised him. (this was later sent by a page). It seems like a festival time, with merchants and many people in the city.

Carrick Silverridge sees Cofisa Tower and tries to get entry, they rebuff him and his group as they are not members of The Tested. They go then to Manor Bridemin, meet Amund Henge and then they learn that the tournament is happening today and that they could participate if they wanted.

The group splits in 2

## The Bathers

Shivon Silverridge Carrick Silverridge, stay behind and have a bath.  After the bath, Shivon Silverridge explores the house and meets Emogene Cosono The head of the tested in the east. She gives an offer for the House Silverridge to take a private test so there will not be any dishonor if they fail and are killed. In a conversation about Leon Blackwood, Emogene Cosono notes the mysterious Sleeping Gryphon a Bellringer mystic, who has the power of the control of reality.

## The Tournament Jousters

Caden Mattix, Cynog Derth, Gregory Ap Banille all join the lists.

- Run 1 - Cynog Derth vs Caden Mattix  Caden Mattix wins
- Run 2 - Gregory ap Bentil vs Raynard Svendsen  Gregory ap Bentil wins
- Run 3 - Caden Mattix vs Archibald Foster  Caden Mattix wins
- Run 4 - Caden Mattix vs Gregory ap Bentil  Gregory ap Bentil wins
- Final Run - Aden Kelmons vs Gregory ap Bentil  Aden Kelmons wins

They learn that there is a tournament in House Pearstone domain In the next few weeks.

After the Tournament the group goes to the local tavern Orange Belltower Inn, They learn a famous minstrel is going to be playing tomorrow named Montross Oldar, eventually Carrick Silverridge comes and gets drunk.

## Dinner at Bridemin Manor

The cadre return with now Carrick Silverridge still drunk is dropped off in a room, as the dinner starts. Other then the members of House Henge there is also Montross Oldar and Emogene Cosono are also in there.

Caden Mattix makes a strong positive impression with Ingrid Henge, possibly starting a relationship.

Elric Henge invites them to go to the horse fields tomorrow.

They learn that the great steed Red Hare was bought for 3 years of tax by Douglas Sherman.

## The Peacekeepers Arrive

Hearing 3 bells, people realize some imperial units are coming in. As the bells go, Montross Oldar sneaks away but is confronted by Cynog Derth and she says that she wishes no will to House Henge but will not wait for the peacekeepers, she will tell Cynog Derth what her story is at the tavern later.

As the Peacekeepers (one named Alfred Brester) come in they also have a man with him, who turns out to be Victor Rameriz.

After some prodding from Emogene Cosono it comes out that Victor Rameriz's goal is to go to Windbreak to tell Connor Silverrige of the Grand Conclave of houses.

The cadre leave to the Orange Belltower Inn to regroup for the next night.
