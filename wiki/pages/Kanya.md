# History

The assassin guild of House Almerin. Originally they were a group of Saltbloods that rejected the compromise given by Ethan Sedi and were prepared to go to war for what was rightfully theirs.

They were pacified by a secret treaty that while they would be denied rulership of the land they would be exempt from crime of murder. While this treaty was never ratified (and likely was never intended to be), it was held as the founding of the Guild of the Kanya, killers for hire.

The Kanya only take the children of people marked to be killed. They raise these children in the backstreets of major cities until they have made their first kill. Then they are taken to the jungles of the south where they must sacrifice what is held that what is most dearly held by them. This loss connects them to either the Peals, or an old god based on their upbringing, this connection tattoos the god on their body, and they normally get a weapon of shadow that they can conjure out of the tattoo. The most common weapon is a garrote/rope.
