This used to be the shining gem of the east. The noble House Henge ruled the for 8 generations, inspiring the east. That was the sunset on the house. With 2 bad generations, and the rise of the pirate lords led by House Ramirez. As Ramirez troops marched on Faemill destroying their ability to resist, with House Henge taking the knee, submitting to newly minted House Silverridge. House Henge's castle was put in to ruin, with only Cofisa Tower left standing as it was a school for the tested of the previous domain of the east. In the later generations House Henge has built up an army, but they still see themselves as the noble line of the east.
