The Iron Veil, a legendary armor birthed from the fiery heart of Nyirborg's forges, stands as an enduring symbol of their craftsmanship and the indomitable spirit that courses through their veins.*

*The heart of Nyirborg's power lies in the resplendent halls of their Forge Sanctum, a sacred space where the rhythmic symphony of hammer on anvil echoes through time. Here, skilled artisans, bound by tradition and a deep-seated connection to their ancestral craft, toil tirelessly to shape weapons and armor that transcend the mundane. The Forge Sanctum is a haven of innovation, where old techniques from the distant lands of Figor, long forgotten in the Belllands, are carefully preserved and incorporated into their masterpieces.*

His hands, calloused and seasoned by the forge, weave magic into every piece that bears the Nyirborg mark. Her creations are often imbued with enchantments that enhance the wearer's strength or resilience, marking her as a beacon of innovation within the house. Lady Isolde's workshop is a place of arcane experimentation, where the boundaries of metallurgical mastery are constantly pushed.*

*Sir Alistair Forgeheart, the house's stalwart champion, is a living testament to the might of Nyirborg craftsmanship. *House Nyirborg's influence extends far beyond the confines of their ancestral stronghold, reaching into the heart of Montimer's military and economic endeavors. The weapons and armor that bear the Nyirborg seal are coveted treasures, sought after by noble houses and knights alike. Yet, amidst the clangor of the forge and the brilliance of their creations, House Nyirborg harbors ancient secrets and a lineage steeped in the traditions of Figor. As the Iron Veil continues to shape the fate of Montimer, House Nyirborg stands resolute, its legacy a testament to the enduring power of craftsmanship and the unyielding spirit of those who call the Forge Sanctum their home.*
