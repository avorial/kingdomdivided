## **House Upperton - Legacy of Bravery and Honor**

*House Upperton, a noble house with roots tracing back to the Holy lands of Figor, boasts a long and storied history defined by bravery, sacrifice, and an unimpeachable sense of honor. When *[[House-Montmier|House Montmier]]* faced exile, House Upperton stood by them, bound not only by blood but also by a shared understanding of destiny influenced by the celestial forces. Landing in the Belllands, they swiftly engaged the *[[Esten-Kings|*Esten Kings*]]*, earning respect for their martial prowess.*

## **Siege of Handfasted**

*Among the numerous tales etched in the annals of House Upperton, the Siege of Handfasted stands out as a testament to their military acumen. Surrounding a sizable *[[Esten-Kings|*Esten King*]]* force, House Upperton engaged in skirmishes that persisted for weeks. The turning point came with the mysterious disappearance of General Ishmael Upperton. The house, suspecting foul play, intensified its efforts, eventually breaching the town's walls after 40 days. Astonishingly, General Ishmael emerged hand in hand with the *[[Esten-Kings|*Esten King*]]*'s general—a woman of striking beauty.*

*General Ishmael, having experienced honorable treatment during his absence, was deeply moved. He believed that the lack of honor in their own treatment of prisoners rendered their victory unjust. Issuing orders to release the prisoners, House Upperton returned home. In an unexpected turn, General Ishmael married the *[[Esten-Kings|*Esten King's*]]* general, forging a connection that would endure for centuries. House Upperton embraced Esten words, creating a unique slang, marking the depth of their integration.*

## **The Aftermath and Cold War**

*The bond between House Upperton and *[[House-Montmier|House Montmier]]* endured through the centuries, marked by a commitment to honor and mutual respect. However, the aftermath of the war saw the *[[House-Almirn|*Almern *]]*and the Esten lands near them become a battleground for a cold war. Border skirmishes and raids fueled animosity between House Upperton and the *[[House-Almirn|*Almern*]]*, creating a simmering tension.*

*Despite the hostility, these conflicts honed warriors on both sides, making them some of the most seasoned in the land. House Upperton consistently took a leading role in these clashes, embodying their unwavering commitment to their beliefs. Warriors from House Upperton have become synonymous with tenacity, skill, and an unyielding spirit.*

## **The Current Era**

*Baron Demetirus Upperton, the current ruler, continues the legacy of his house. Married to Xena, an Esten, the union symbolizes an enduring bridge between the two cultures. Their children, Peter and Clarissa, represent the next generation of House Upperton. Peter, recently elevated to the Duke of Pearstone, shoulders the responsibility of continuing the house's martial tradition.*
