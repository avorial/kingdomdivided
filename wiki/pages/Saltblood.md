# History

In the mystic tapestry of history, an enigma persists—the Saltblood, a people without a cultural lineage, a race that remains detached from the heritage of the great civilizations that once flourished. Their origins trace back to an epoch shrouded in the veils of time, a period known as the Heartbeat, where the Conference of Gods convened to address a dire threat.

As the Coronaris pulsed with tremors threatening cataclysm, representatives from diverse realms gathered their mightiest beings to embark on a perilous quest. The mission was ambitious yet critical: delve into the depths of the underworlds veins, locate the heart, and silence the rhythmic quakes that threatened to rend the very fabric of existence.

For years, they delved into the subterranean realms, navigating treacherous passages in pursuit of the elusive heart. The resonance of their efforts echoed through the land until, at last, they achieved success. The heart's relentless throb ceased, and in its wake, a torrent of newly awakened blood surged forth. The blood, in its divine essence, flowed unbridled, cascading toward the sea with an unstoppable force. Within this crimson tide emerged the bodies of those who had died valiantly in the fight of the great war against the heart of Oh.

To the amazement of all, these were not lifeless corpses but reborn entities, individuals devoid of memories or allegiance to their erstwhile kingdoms. Cast adrift upon the sea, they called themselves Saltbloods, and they found themselves on small islands, surrounded by the vast expanse of the ocean. In their shared journey of rediscovery, they grappled with the fragments of their past, sharing whispered memories and fragments of a time that had been washed away.

Building boats, the Saltblood set forth to reunite with other survivors scattered across the expanse of the seas. Choosing to inhabit uninhabited islands, they formed small, close-knit communities, each a haven for those seeking solace in shared remembrances. The Saltblood, with no ancestral ties to the grand civilizations of old, became a people united by the enigma of their rebirth and the memories that lingered on the fringes of their consciousness.

Over time they realized that the touch of the blood of the heartbeat had changed them,  as magics became common among their people.

As they navigated the uncharted waters of their existence, the Saltblood clung to the fragments of history and the echoes of battles waged in the depths of the earth. They forged a culture from the remnants of a forgotten era, a society defined by resilience, adaptability, and the shared mystery of their origin. The Saltblood, in their collective journey, became a testament to the enduring spirit of those who emerged from the Heartbeat, reborn into a world anew.
