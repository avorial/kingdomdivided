# The Tested

On the morning after the raid to free the captives, a new group arrives. Shockingly, it is a new Ramirez. Boris Ramirez is a Tested, and accompanied a group of Durand soldiers commanded by Tristan Boucher who traveled to join Casimir Durand. Boris meets the cadre as well as Conner, and is welcomed in, despite the surprise that a Tested would join the notorious Silverridges.

## A Monstrous Trade

Caden decides to bring the new Tested and everyone (minus Casimir, who is feuding with the King of the Bells) to meet with Nola Holt to trade his war beetle larva. He receives a magical seed that will protect him once within the next year. Now they have a Tested in their cadre, it seems they are less welcome in the Halls of the Tested. After returning Caden tells Conner. As is often the case, Casimir is unhappy with the lack of communication to what he sees to be a military manner.

# Dangerous Infiltration and a Surprising Revelation

The cadre decides to take advantage of intelligence that suggests there is a Saffron meeting in a windmill. They arrive posing as mercenary pirates, wanting to join the Saffrons. Most are disarmed, though Amond is able to keep his weapons, and Lyla just removes the string to her bow. When asked if they want to merely be paid or go for the long haul, they wisely choose the long haul. While there, Caden angers Lyla by bringing up the Shadow Guards.   
  
While there, they notice an Imperial horse, suggesting that someone in direct employ of the Empire is present. The cadre briefly strategizes what their goal will be, and while Casimir pushes for a more aggressive plan, the consensus is that a more modest goal of infiltration is better suited, with the ability to ramp up as needed.

After they are shown their rooms for the night, Lyla sneaks out. She scouts out the land, and sees two shocking figures: Sofyia Baranova, a high ranking Peacekeeper, meeting with Samson Kreeg.
