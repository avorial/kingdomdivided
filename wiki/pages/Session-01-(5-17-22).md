## The Meeting with the Duke

The first game, the characters meet with the Duke [[connor_silverridge|Silverridge]], and he tells you now due to disruptions in the banners that the party must make a more active effort in improving the domain. After that they go to a local pub and see the level of disparity with the poor and the nobles. This leads to a fight the players win easily. After that Sir Rory tells them the disposition of the diplomats.
