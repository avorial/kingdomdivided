While every child knows of Oh, from the star gods tears, and the worm gods breaking him up. Only the educated understand how each of the parts of Oh, became the spheres in the night sky.

As the worms broke apart Oh, the head separated and the screams of pain in the darkness ignited. The light of that fire will burn until the next Oh devours him. The hands and the feet broke off, and by legend the chest was opened up and the heart ripped out. As the limbs flayed in the ether, the worm gods pushed and pulled. Over centuries the limbs lost their forms and became spheres.

Eons upon eons past as the Star Gods trepidation waned seeing that Oh no longer could devour them they returned dancing in a circle over the corpse, being the constellations we see in the night sky. The worm gods had succeeded in taking down Oh, and kept their devouring to a minimum, as the Old Gods awoke from the body, making the earth, wind, water and fire..

The current details of the the spheres, only comes from Society of Letters research, or Leadan explorers who look to the night sky.

Solar System

- Cestona “Sun” - The Screaming Face - too brilliant to stare directly at the flaming head of Oh, it constantly tries to bring it self together. Impotently trying to get his body together, only the plants and the trees rise up to its call.
- Manathara “The Winter Traveller” - The left hand, carved to a bluish crystal. During the colder months, on the Coronaris the nights are illuminated by Mantahara's reflection.
- Zephyria “The Summer Traveller” - The right hand, giving a reddish blood tone when it follows Coronaris during the warmer months. Known as the warrior months, it's seen as a time for violence and warfare.
- Coronaris “The Heartstone” from the heart of Oh will come it's future. Originally home to the great Kadal empire though the apoloypitic heartbeat the countries were broken in to great nations.
- Vastoria “The Core, Chest” the huge sphere, large in the sky, during some years so bright that night is as bright as day. Leadans have seen roads and great fires on its surface. While the Tested suppress any rumors of movement on it's surface.
- Astroins “The Memory” - The left leg, is a larger sphere with rings around it, green, with rivers running though it, some feel it's where the Old Gods have thrived and made kingdoms of grandeur.
- Gaialith “The Messenger” - The right leg, and brother to Astronis, also having rings, is a desert world with glass spires visible even from Coronaris. Many feel it's the staging grounds for the Star Gods.
