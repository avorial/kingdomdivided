# The Treaty of Fire and Waves

On this day the 23rd day of Rainmarrow three great dukes agree to the following.

*Act One: Carrick Silverridge and Myo Songprawati first female born heir will inherit the titles and power of the domain of Silverridge.*

*Act Two: Timothy White-star and Shivon Silverridge first male born heir will inherit titles and power of the domain of Whitestar.*

*Act Three: These children or the children who would inherit, if a child is found unable to have children will marry. Their union at the youngests 18th's nameday will form the Archdutcy of Silverstar.*

*Act Four: The first male child of this union of the Archdutchy of Silverstar, will marry the highest ranking female Almirn heir at that youngests 18th nameday. At the birth of that union's child, The child will inherit the dutchy of Almirn and unify in to the Kingdom of the East.*

*Act Five: This treaty is null and void if a child is not born in 5 years after the marriage of anyone named in title or spirit of this Treaty or if a child of the appropriate gender is not born to further the treaty.*

*Act Six: This treaty is broken if any of it's members fails to come to aid one another if called, or forgive the Bellringer if one of the houses here agresses against one another by act or treaty.  *

*Act Seven: None of these precepts here will be made public to the imperial regime, and in such silence may we not be seen as traitors to our oaths to our leige. If exposed this treaty will be made null and void as we are all supporters of the imperial house who has invested us with the titles we hold today,*

*Act Eight: All the undersigned, do so with an open heart, and with the desire for unity. If manipulation or lies are found to be it's foundation this treaty will be broken before it began.*

Duke Timothy White-Star

Duke Conner Silverridge

Duke Salazar Almirn
