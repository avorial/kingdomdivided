# History:

House Wycliff: Masters of the Sea and Power Players of the East

House Wycliff, the formidable banner within the domain of House Silverridge, has a captivating history that intertwines piracy, political influence, and a deep connection to the sea. Led by the charismatic and cunning Aiden Wycliff, the house has risen to prominence, earning a Count Title and overshadowing other banners within the region, who hold lesser titles of barons.

From its very inception under Robert Wycliff, House Wycliff has been deeply rooted in maritime affairs. As Emir Ramirez took control of the domain of the east, the Wycliffs saw an opportunity to establish themselves in the coastal city of Estrage, a strategic hub for their seafaring endeavors. Known for their piratical pursuits, House Wycliff quickly became synonymous with daring raids, audacious heists, and the accumulation of wealth through both legal and illegal means.

The symbol of House Wycliff, the golden hand, has become a feared and recognized emblem throughout the realm. It serves as a stark warning to those who would cross their path, representing their firm grip on power, their golden touch when it comes to acquiring riches, and their unwavering determination to protect their interests at any cost.

The private police force of House Wycliff, the Gold Blades, stands as a formidable and disciplined unit, instilling order and maintaining the house's authority. Armed with gleaming golden swords, they patrol the streets of Estrage, ensuring that House Wycliff's rule is respected and that their operations remain covert and protected. Their presence strikes fear into the hearts of both common folk and rival factions, further solidifying House Wycliff's influence.

Beyond their piratical pursuits, House Wycliff understands the importance of legacy and succession. Aiden Wycliff has taken great care to ensure that the next generation is well-prepared to carry on the family's legacy. Decker Wycliff, their son, has proven himself as one of the most skilled and respected captains on the open seas, commanding a formidable fleet that has bolstered House Wycliff's maritime dominance.

Meanwhile, their daughter Terresa Wycliff has forged her own path as a masterful spymaster. Learning from her mother's expertise and guidance, Terresa has cultivated a vast network of contacts across the eastern side of the Bell Lands. Her skills in intelligence gathering, manipulation, and espionage have solidified House Wycliff's position as both a force to be reckoned with on the seas and a formidable presence in the political arena.

In matters of faith, House Wycliff adheres to ancient traditions passed down from the first conquering peoples. Rejecting the worship of most gods, they place their trust in their own cunning, resourcefulness, and strategic acumen during times of hardship. However, they recognize the power of faith and superstition as potent tools of propaganda, weaving them skillfully into their dealings with both allies and adversaries.

House Wycliff, with their pirate roots, political prowess, and unwavering determination, has firmly established itself as a force that cannot be ignored. Their mastery of the seas, wealth accumulation, and careful cultivation of alliances make them a significant power player in the realm. With Aiden Wycliff's leadership, supported by the impressive skills of Decker and Terresa, House Wycliff's influence continues to expand, leaving an indelible mark on the history of the Bell Lands.

Crest: The heraldry of House Wycliff, is a red and white, with a golden hand in the middle. The red and white symbolize blood and sea foam, going back to their mastery of the waters. The golden hand used to symbolize that to hold wealth you must master the blood and foam, but now they are a landed house many feel it is a symbol of their harsh justice given to the poor.

# Secrets:

1. The Lost Treasure: House Wycliff guards a closely-held secret regarding a legendary lost treasure hidden somewhere along the treacherous coastlines they once frequented as pirates. The knowledge of this treasure, believed to be an immense fortune amassed over generations, has been passed down through the Wycliff family. However, the exact location and nature of the treasure remain elusive, known only to a select few within the house. Its discovery could potentially solidify House Wycliff's wealth and power, or become a coveted prize for rivals seeking to unravel the mystery.
2. Shadow Alliances: While House Wycliff maintains a public image as a powerful banner within the domain of House Silverridge, they secretly maintain strategic alliances with traders in Kadal the Undying Lands. Through these shadow alliance, House Wycliff gains access to illicit resources, intelligence networks, and additional muscle to protect their interests. These covert connections enable them to navigate the murky waters of political intrigue and maintain an iron grip on their power, even in the face of opposition.
3. A Hidden Naval Arsenal: Off on the Isle of Sanctuary House Wycliff has a hidden naval arsenal, concealed from prying eyes. This clandestine facility serves as a well-guarded secret, housing a fleet of advanced warships and state-of-the-art naval technology. The arsenal provides House Wycliff with a significant military advantage, as their naval forces are more formidable than they publicly reveal. The secret arsenal allows them to strike swiftly and decisively in conflicts that threaten their domain or maritime interests, catching their enemies off guard and ensuring their dominance at sea.
4. The young daughter of House Wycliff was taken hostage by the imperial seat, and was only rescued by the support of House Silverridge.

# NPC's:

1. Captain Elena Stormrider: Born into House Wycliff, Captain Elena Stormrider has inherited her family's seafaring legacy. As a skilled navigator and fearless captain, she commands one of House Wycliff's most formidable pirate ships, known as The Tempest's Fury. Elena's loyalty to her house is unwavering, and she is known for her fierce determination to protect House Wycliff's interests at sea. With a reputation for cunning strategies and unmatched knowledge of the treacherous coastlines, Captain Elena Stormrider is a force to be reckoned with on the high seas.
2. Marcus Blackthorn, the Underworld Broker: Marcus Blackthorn serves as House Wycliff's covert liaison to the underworld. With a network of informants and connections in various criminal organizations, Marcus is the go-to person for securing illicit goods, information, and favors. Born into a family of skilled smugglers, Marcus has honed his skills in navigating the shadowy underbelly of society. His ability to broker deals and maintain discretion has earned him a position of trust within House Wycliff, making him a valuable asset in their operations.
3. Lady Isabella Thornwood, Mistress of Intrigue: Lady Isabella Thornwood, childhood friend of [[terresa_wycliff|Terresa Wycliff]], is known for her mastery of intrigue and espionage. A strategic thinker and manipulative tactician, she utilizes her charm and wit to gather intelligence and secure House Wycliff's position in the political landscape. Lady Isabella has a vast web of spies and informants, extending her reach across the eastern side of the Bell Lands. Her knowledge of rival houses, hidden agendas, and secret alliances makes her an invaluable asset to House Wycliff, ensuring their survival in a realm filled with deceit and power struggles. She is the right hand to [[terresa_wycliff|Terresa Wycliff]] in all matters dealing with the pomegranates.
