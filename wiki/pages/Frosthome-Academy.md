While some nobles view attendance at Frostholm as a mere formality, spending their time indulging in the city's infamous brothels and revelry, there are others who recognize the true value of the education that awaits them within the hallowed halls of the academy.

Within the castle's grand halls, students from noble families across the realm gather to embark on a journey of knowledge and self-discovery. The vast libraries of Frostholm Academy house an extensive collection of ancient tomes and scrolls, a treasure trove of knowledge awaiting eager minds. Here, students immerse themselves in the pursuit of wisdom, delving into the depths of arcane theory, unraveling the mysteries of history, and uncovering the secrets of forgotten civilizations.
