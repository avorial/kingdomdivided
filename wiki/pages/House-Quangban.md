House Quangban, originating as one of the original captains affiliated with House Almern, was assigned the challenging task of taming the deepest jungles within the new domain. This unexplored territory harbored enigmatic creatures, some of colossal proportions, unseen anywhere else in the realm. Unfortunately, these gigantic beasts took a heavy toll on House Quangban, relegating them from the ranks of key nobility. The rugged terrain, once explored, led the creatures to venture beyond its boundaries, causing decades of attacks on nearby domains until they were either contained or eradicated.

Despite the losses, House Quangban contributed significantly to House Almern by providing them with tamed dragons. However, they retained the more formidable and dangerous specimens for themselves. Over time, the rationale behind the presence of such monstrous creatures in their domain became clear — ancient caves leading to the core of the heartstone were discovered. These heartstone caves are a source of profound mystery and power, and House Quangban has taken it upon themselves to guard them vigilantly, employing the very creatures they have tamed to protect these enigmatic realms. The Primeval Jungle, under their watchful eye, stands as a testament to both the perilous challenges faced by House Quangban and the untapped wonders hidden within their domain. As the Guardians of the Primeval Jungle, they navigate a delicate balance between the ferocity of the jungle's denizens and the protection of the ancient secrets nestled within its heartstone caves.
