# History

In the rich and storied history of the Belllands, a tale of exiled nobles, conquest, and the rise of a new Emperor unfolds. It all began with a group of noble houses who staunchly supported the Bellringer, a figure of rebellion and resistance against the Holy State of Fygor. These houses, namely House Covenguard, House Gratsi, and House Montimer, were so unwavering in their allegiance to the Bellringer that when his coup to seize the country failed, they were cast out and exiled from their homeland.

Undeterred by their exile, these noble houses set their sights on a new domain—the realm of the Esten Kings. Over the course of two centuries, they waged relentless battles against the Esten kings, fueled by their unwavering determination and a thirst for justice. Through strategic prowess, valor on the battlefield, and unwavering loyalty to their cause, these noble houses gradually gained the upper hand.

Amidst the tumultuous conflicts, a figure of great leadership emerged—Emperor Ethan Sedi. With House Whitestar as his steadfast allies, Emperor Ethan Sedi rallied the disparate forces of the Belllands and united them under a single banner. Together, they formed a formidable alliance that would challenge the dominance of the Esten kings.

Through a series of hard-fought battles, political maneuverings, and strategic alliances, the Belllands gradually gained ground against their adversaries. The united forces under Emperor Ethan Sedi's command proved to be a formidable force, and the Esten kings found themselves increasingly cornered.

Finally, at the rising of a new era, the Esten kings were defeated, and the Belllands celebrated their hard-won victory. The once-exiled noble houses, now firmly established as pillars of the Belllands, reclaimed their rightful place in the realm they had fought so fiercely to protect. Their resilience, determination, and unwavering loyalty to the Bellringer's cause had paved the way for a new chapter in the history of the Belllands.

As the Belllands entered a new era under the rule of Emperor Ethan Sedi, the foundations were laid for a realm that would flourish and grow.

After the glorious reign of the Sedi dynasty that spanned three centuries, a dark turn of events unfolded within the Belllands. One of the prominent banners that had stood alongside the Sedis for generations turned traitorous, launching a treacherous coup and seizing control of the realm. This marked the dawn of a new era known as the Rivers Empire, named after the usurping banner.

The betrayal inflicted a severe blow to the unity and stability that the Belllands had enjoyed under the Sedi dynasty. The Rivers Empire sought to consolidate its power and exert control over the vast territories of the realm, imposing its own brand of governance and reshaping the political landscape. The once-unified Belllands now found itself divided, with loyalists to the Sedi dynasty scattered and marginalized.

In this turbulent period, notable houses and societies rose to prominence, shaping the course of history in the Belllands. House Bellkind emerged as a powerful noble house, its influence spreading across the realm. Renowned for their strategic brilliance, martial prowess, and intricate political machinations, House Bellkind played a pivotal role in the ongoing power struggles within the Belllands.

House Pearstone, another prominent noble house, established its seat of power in the heart of the realm. Known for their wealth, influence, and extensive trading networks, House Pearstone thrived in the midst of the political upheaval, positioning themselves as key players in the economic landscape of the Belllands.

Amidst the chaos, a clandestine society known as the Society of Letters, also referred to as the Tested, emerged as a force to be reckoned with. Composed of scholars, intellectuals, and individuals with a thirst for knowledge, the Society of Letters became a hub of intellectual discourse, secret knowledge, and clandestine activities. They worked diligently to preserve ancient wisdom, challenge the oppressive rule of the Rivers Empire, and foster a sense of enlightenment within the realm.

As the Rivers Empire sought to tighten its grip on power, the Belllands became a hotbed of intrigue, conflict, and resistance. The various factions vied for control, maneuvering behind the scenes, engaging in covert operations, and forming alliances to challenge the Rivers Empire's dominance.

The age of the Rivers Empire marked a tumultuous chapter in the history of the Belllands, defined by shifting loyalties, political maneuverings, and the emergence of powerful houses and societies. It was a time of both turmoil and opportunity, as the realm teetered on the brink of uncertainty, awaiting the rise of new heroes, and the potential for a resurgence of the Sedi legacy.
